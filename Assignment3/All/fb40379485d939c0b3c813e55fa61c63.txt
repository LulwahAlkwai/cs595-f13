<div class="shift-to-hero"><p><iframe allowfullscreen="" frameborder="0" height="360" src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/hsEoVeEwbvw" width="640"></iframe></p></div>
<p><a href="http://mashable.com/category/ios-7/">iOS 7</a> dropped Wednesday, and with it comes arguably the biggest overhaul to iOS since its debut back in 2007.</p>
<p>The biggest change in iOS 7 is its complete visual overhaul. Every pixel was redrawn and backgrounds now have a parallax effect, making the pixels and icons look as though they're floating on top of the screen. </p>
<div class="see-also"><p>See also: <a href="http://mashable.com/2013/09/17/what-to-do-before-ios-7/" data-crackerjax="#post-slider">What to Do Before Downloading iOS 7</a></p></div>
<p>Multitasking is improved, too, with a webOS-like card view. Universal search from any page now exists on the launcher. The new OS also boasts new features like Control Center and AirDrop.</p>
<p>Check out our brief overview of some of the big changes in iOS 7 and stay tuned for our full review and continued coverage over the next few days.</p>
