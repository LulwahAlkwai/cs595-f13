Alleged Gold iPhone 5S Housing Reveals New Flash

   1.5k
   Shares
   [44]Share
   [45]Tweet
   [46]Share
   What's This?
   Iphone5skeatonkeller
   [47]Vignesh
   By Vignesh Ramachandran2013-08-29 03:30:57 UTC

   A new [48]YouTube video claims to show the back housing for the rumored
   gold [49]iPhone 5S, providing some clues about what Apple might release
   next.

   [50]Keaton Keller, of YouTube channel [51]TechSmartt, showed the back
   housing for what he claims is Apple's next-generation iPhone. The
   housing is a gold-champagne color, and looks similar in size to the
   iPhone 5.

   See also: [52]7 iPhone Concepts That'll Make You Salivate

   In his video, embedded below, Keller notes that only the flash appears
   changed on the new 5S (it's now rectangular). The headphone jack and
   Lightning port remain on the bottom, and the volume rockers and mute
   switch have the same design as they do on the 5.

   IFRAME: [53]//www.youtube.com/embed/13iAc0FXQLc?rel=0

   Since it isn't clear where Keller obtained the phone housing, Mashable
   is unable to verify the authenticity of his claims. [54]Apple has not
   publicly acknowledged the iPhone rumors, but the Cupertino, Calif.
   company is [55]expected to unveil new high-end and low-end iPhone
   models next month. Apple had not responded to Mashable's written
   request for comment, as of Thursday afternoon.

   There are also [56]reports that the iPhone 5S could come in a graphite
   color.

   What do you think of the video's findings? Sound off in the comments,
   below.
