10% of American Internet Users Shop on Smartphones

   2.5k
   Shares
   [44]Share
   [45]Tweet
   [46]Share
   What's This?
   Ascani_ipad_02
   [47]Photo
   By Zoe Fox2013-09-16 23:05:20 UTC

   Americans lag behind Chinese Internet users when it comes to mobile
   shopping. Nearly twice as many Chinese Internet users (18%) shop via
   their mobile phones as Americans (10%).

   [48]Statista's chart, below, uses data from [49]RetailMeNot and
   [50]Ipsos to show the prevalence of online shopping in 10 countries.

   See also: [51]7 Surprising Facts About Mobile Shoppers

   In France, for example, just 2% of adult Internet users shop on their
   mobiles. It's a similar story in Canada and Italy, where just 4% engage
   in mobile shopping.

   2013_09_16_Shopping

   Do you primarily shop on your smartphone, or do you prefer sitting down
   at your laptop? Tell us in the comments below.
