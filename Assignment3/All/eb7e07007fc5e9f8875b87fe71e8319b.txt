<section class='article-content'>

<div class="shift-to-hero"><p><iframe allowfullscreen="" frameborder="0" height="360" src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/SbGpjrHcsIk" width="640"></iframe></p></div>
<p>Sometimes, your <a href="http://mashable.com/category/instagram/">Instagram</a> feed resembles a virtual dessert cart with sweet treats clogging up the photo stream. But one dessert actually deserves a social media shout-out. </p>
<p>Feast your sugar-craving eyes on this Instagram cake from <em><a href="http://www.howtocookthat.net/public_html/instagram-cake/" target="_blank">How to Cook That</a></em>. </p>
<div class="see-also"><p>See also: <a href="http://mashable.com/2013/09/04/vine-instagram-special-effects/" data-crackerjax="#post-slider">How to Add Special Effects to Vine and Instagram Videos</a></p></div>
<p>Ann Riordan, the blogger behind <em>How to Cook That</em>, demonstrates how to bake a chocolate mousse cake that looks like Instagram's logo. In the <a href="http://mashable.com/category/youtube/" target="_blank">YouTube</a> video above, she walks bakers step-by-step through the process. </p>
<p>While the recipe is time consuming and a bit advanced for the novice chef, the finished product is worth it. This cake is perfect for the most social-savvy friends in your stream. </p>
