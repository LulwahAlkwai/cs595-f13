Watch This Woman Age Decades in Just 5 Minutes

   11.0k
   Shares
   [44]Share
   [45]Tweet
   [46]Share
   What's This?
   [47]Laura%20vitto%20(1)
   By Laura Vitto2013-09-11 05:00:30 UTC

   Looking in the mirror every day, it's unlikely that we notice our faces
   aging. It isn't until we look back at [48]photos that we see how much
   faces can change in just a few years.

   See also: [49]Travel the World With These 10 Time Lapse Videos

   Filmmaker [50]Anthony Cerniello explores this idea in his latest short
   film, Danielle. The simple clip focuses on a woman's face before a
   white backdrop. In under five minutes, the character ages decades, from
   a very young child to an older woman.

   IFRAME: [51]//player.vimeo.com/video/74033442

   Cerniello told Mashable that the video is based around a friend,
   Danielle, and photographs of her cousins and relatives. After scanning
   the individual photographs, he searched for similarities in bone
   structure among the women.

   Cerniello created a slow morph of these still in Adobe After Effects,
   and hired animators to add in blinks, mouth and neck gestures and
   camera movements. After a nuke artist smoothed out discrepancies in the
   hair and eyes and color corrected between skin tones, Cerniello
   finished the piece with eerie abstract music, composed by a friend,
   Mark Reveley.

   "I was thinking a lot about death, having panic attacks for a while and
   I wanted to explore that, I guess," says Cerniello. "It was my way of
   working through that time of my life."
