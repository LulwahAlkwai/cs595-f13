<p>In honor of the twelfth anniversary of the <a href="http://mashable.com/category/september-11/">Sept. 11</a> attacks, <a href="http://www.youtube.com/user/earthcam?feature=watch" target="_blank">Earth Cam</a> has pieced together a <a href="http://mashable.com/category/time-lapse/">time-lapse</a> video showing the progress of One World Trade Center in New York City. </p>
<p>The video features striking footage from October 2004 to September 2013, and captures the completion of the tower, including the addition of the spire last May. </p>
