<div class="shift-to-hero"><p><iframe allowfullscreen="" frameborder="0" height="360" src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/p8IAkbWJNfY" width="640"></iframe></p></div>
<p><a href="http://mashable.com/category/apple/">Apple</a> has launched the first ad for its "budget" smartphone, the <a href="http://mashable.com/category/iphone-5c/">iPhone 5C</a>. </p>
<p>Titled "Plastic Perfected" and accompanied by a tune from Sleigh Bells, the app shows the elements of the iPhone 5C being "molded" from liquid plastic. </p>
<p>The caption of the ad says "A completely new expression of iPhone that's not just for lovers of color. It's for the colorful."</p>
<div class="see-also"><p>See also: <a href="http://mashable.com/2013/09/10/things-iphone-5c-looks-like/" data-crackerjax="#post-slider">10 Things the iPhone 5C Looks Like</a></p></div>
<p>The iPhone 5C is Apple's $99 smartphone (with a contract), and although it doesn't have the <a href="http://mashable.com/2013/09/10/iphone-5s-hands-on/" data-crackerjax="#post-slider">iPhone 5S</a>' fingerprint scanner and is made out of plastic, it's nearly identical, at a component-level, to the iPhone 5. </p>
<p>How do you like the ad? Share your thoughts in the comments. </p>

</header><ol class="slides">
<li class="slide" data-content-source="Gallery - Default" data-id="523859865198401f9e00ed5e" data-skip-rerender="false" data-thumb="http://rack.1.mshcdn.com/media/ZgkyMDEzLzA5LzEwLzFlL2FwcGxlZXZlbnQ1LjY5YzUyLmpwZwpwCXRodW1iCTE4MHgxNDAjCmUJanBn/d45878a0/d2c/apple-event-54.jpg">
<figure><img alt="Apple-event-54" src="http://rack.0.mshcdn.com/media/ZgkyMDEzLzA5LzEwLzFlL2FwcGxlZXZlbnQ1LjY5YzUyLmpwZwpwCXRodW1iCTg1MHg1OTA-CmUJanBn/101abb41/d2c/apple-event-54.jpg"></figure><div class="meta">
<h2 class="title">Introducing the iPhone 5C</h2>
<div class="caption"><p>For the first time ever, Apple launched a second device alongside its next-generation iPhone (the 5S). Dubbed the iPhone 5C, the low-cost smartphone will cost $99 in the U.S. for a 16 GB model and $199 for 32 GB.</p></div>
<div class="credit">

</div>
</div>
</li>
<li class="slide" data-content-source="Gallery - Default" data-id="523859865198401f9e00ed5f" data-skip-rerender="false" data-thumb="http://rack.1.mshcdn.com/media/ZgkyMDEzLzA5LzEwL2RmL2FwcGxlZXZlbnQ4LjdlY2RiLmpwZwpwCXRodW1iCTE4MHgxNDAjCmUJanBn/3ebdb4c5/22c/apple-event-81.jpg">
<figure><img alt="Apple-event-81" src="http://rack.0.mshcdn.com/media/ZgkyMDEzLzA5LzEwL2RmL2FwcGxlZXZlbnQ4LjdlY2RiLmpwZwpwCXRodW1iCTg1MHg1OTA-CmUJanBn/4aa1d042/22c/apple-event-81.jpg"></figure><div class="meta">
<h2 class="title">Five Colors</h2>
<div class="caption"><p>The iPhone 5C comes in five flavors: blue, green, yellow, pink and white. </p></div>
<div class="credit">

</div>
</div>
</li>
<li class="slide" data-content-source="Gallery - Default" data-id="523859865198401f9e00ed60" data-skip-rerender="false" data-thumb="http://rack.0.mshcdn.com/media/ZgkyMDEzLzA5LzEwLzIyL2FwcGxlZXZlbnQxLjAxMjg1LmpwZwpwCXRodW1iCTE4MHgxNDAjCmUJanBn/33a4c466/1f5/apple-event-127.jpg">
<figure><img alt="Apple-event-127" src="http://rack.3.mshcdn.com/media/ZgkyMDEzLzA5LzEwLzIyL2FwcGxlZXZlbnQxLjAxMjg1LmpwZwpwCXRodW1iCTg1MHg1OTA-CmUJanBn/50f9129b/1f5/apple-event-127.jpg"></figure><div class="meta">
<h2 class="title">Measurements</h2>
<div class="caption"><p>The iPhone 5C has the same measurements as the iPhone 5 and 5S, but it's constructed from soft silicon rubber, rather than anodized aluminum. </p></div>
<div class="credit">

</div>
</div>
</li>
<li class="slide" data-content-source="Gallery - Default" data-id="523859865198401f9e00ed61" data-skip-rerender="false" data-thumb="http://rack.0.mshcdn.com/media/ZgkyMDEzLzA5LzEwLzIyL2FwcGxlZXZlbnQxLjU0ZjNjLmpwZwpwCXRodW1iCTE4MHgxNDAjCmUJanBn/da3c07a7/dab/apple-event-125.jpg">
<figure><img alt="Apple-event-125" src="http://rack.0.mshcdn.com/media/ZgkyMDEzLzA5LzEwLzIyL2FwcGxlZXZlbnQxLjU0ZjNjLmpwZwpwCXRodW1iCTg1MHg1OTA-CmUJanBn/fe705d70/dab/apple-event-125.jpg"></figure><div class="meta">
<h2 class="title">iOS 7</h2>
<div class="caption"><p>The iPhone 5C will ship with iOS 7, Apple's completely re-engineered mobile operating system. The device also includes an A6 chip and a slightly larger battery than the iPhone 5.</p></div>
<div class="credit">

</div>
</div>
</li>
<li class="slide" data-content-source="Gallery - Default" data-id="523859865198401f9e00ed62" data-skip-rerender="false" data-thumb="http://rack.1.mshcdn.com/media/ZgkyMDEzLzA5LzEwL2E2L2FwcGxlZXZlbnQ3LjI5N2VjLmpwZwpwCXRodW1iCTE4MHgxNDAjCmUJanBn/b662744c/37f/apple-event-77.jpg">
<figure><img alt="Apple-event-77" src="http://rack.0.mshcdn.com/media/ZgkyMDEzLzA5LzEwL2E2L2FwcGxlZXZlbnQ3LjI5N2VjLmpwZwpwCXRodW1iCTg1MHg1OTA-CmUJanBn/aad847ed/37f/apple-event-77.jpg"></figure><div class="meta">
<h2 class="title">iSight Camera</h2>
<div class="caption"><p>The iPhone 5C's iSight camera touts a 3x video zoom and a new FaceTime HD camera. The rear camera is 8 megapixels, and comes with back-side illumination and a hybrid IR filter. It also supports more LTE bands than any other smartphone in the world.</p></div>
<div class="credit">

</div>
</div>
</li>
<li class="slide" data-content-source="Gallery - Default" data-id="523859865198401f9e00ed63" data-skip-rerender="false" data-thumb="http://rack.1.mshcdn.com/media/ZgkyMDEzLzA5LzEwLzIyL2FwcGxlZXZlbnQxLjQ4ZWI4LmpwZwpwCXRodW1iCTE4MHgxNDAjCmUJanBn/040d9c20/4e5/apple-event-178.jpg">
<figure><img alt="Apple-event-178" src="http://rack.3.mshcdn.com/media/ZgkyMDEzLzA5LzEwLzIyL2FwcGxlZXZlbnQxLjQ4ZWI4LmpwZwpwCXRodW1iCTg1MHg1OTA-CmUJanBn/430c395c/4e5/apple-event-178.jpg"></figure><div class="meta">
<h2 class="title">Cases</h2>
<div class="caption"><p>For $29, iPhone 5C users can dress up their devices with brightly colored cases. The cases come with holes to add color-contrasting to the device. Some say it looks like the game Connect Four or other <a href="http://mashable.com/2013/09/10/things-iphone-5c-looks-like/">quirky items</a>.</p></div>
<div class="credit">

</div>
</div>
</li>
<li class="slide" data-content-source="Gallery - Default" data-id="523859865198401f9e00ed64" data-skip-rerender="false" data-thumb="http://rack.2.mshcdn.com/media/ZgkyMDEzLzA5LzEwLzk1L2FwcGxlZXZlbnQyLjc4NDVhLmpwZwpwCXRodW1iCTE4MHgxNDAjCmUJanBn/f585cc71/7ed/apple-event-29.jpg">
<figure><img alt="Apple-event-29" src="http://rack.2.mshcdn.com/media/ZgkyMDEzLzA5LzEwLzk1L2FwcGxlZXZlbnQyLjc4NDVhLmpwZwpwCXRodW1iCTg1MHg1OTA-CmUJanBn/7751185e/7ed/apple-event-29.jpg"></figure><div class="meta">
<h2 class="title">Availability</h2>
<div class="caption"><p>Pre-orders start on Sept. 13, and the iPhone 5C will go on sale on Sept. 20 in the United States, China, Australia, Canada, France, Germany, Japan, Singapore and the United Kingdom. By December, the iPhone 5C will be available in over 100 countries and on 270 carriers.</p></div>
<div class="credit">

</div>
</div>
</li>
