	<h1 class="entry-title"><a href="http://proprtips.com/2011/10/25/tip-185-i-can-handle-the-truth/" rel="bookmark">Tip #185: I can handle the&nbsp;truth</a></h1>
	<div class="entry entry-content">
		<div id="attachment_1027" class="wp-caption alignnone" style="width: 510px"><a href="http://proprtips.files.wordpress.com/2011/10/seenwhere.jpg"><img src="http://proprtips.files.wordpress.com/2011/10/seenwhere.jpg?w=500&#038;h=70" alt="" title="Says you" width="500" height="70" class="size-full wp-image-1027" /></a><p class="wp-caption-text">&quot;As seen in&quot; element on a marketing site. Just graphics, no links. Big mistake.</p></div>
<p>If, on the marketing site you want me to see, you have &#8220;As seen in&#8230;&#8221; graphics pointing to other coverage you&#8217;ve gotten, make sure you link to the coverage you seem to be so proud of. Otherwise, for all I know, the coverage was glancing, negative, or perhaps nonexistent.</p>
<p>But also keep in mind: <a href="http://proprtips.com/2008/10/13/tip-49-how-not-to-pitch">Tip #49: How not to pitch</a>.</p>
