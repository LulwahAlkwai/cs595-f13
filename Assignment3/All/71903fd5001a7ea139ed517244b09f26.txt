<h1>Physics > Physics and Society</h1>

<h1 class="title"><span class="descriptor">Title:</span>
Qualified Trust, not Surveillance, is the Basis of a Stable Society</h1>
<div class="authors"><span class="descriptor">Authors:</span>
<a href="/find/physics/1/au:+Helbing_D/0/1/0/all/0/1">Dirk Helbing</a></div>
<div class="dateline">(Submitted on 8 Jun 2013)</div>
<blockquote class="abstract">
<span class="descriptor">Abstract:</span> Peaceful citizens and hard-working taxpayers are under government
surveillance. Confidential communication of journalists is intercepted.
Civilians are killed by drones, without a chance to prove their innocence. How
could it come that far? And what are the alternatives?
</blockquote>
