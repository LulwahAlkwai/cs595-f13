<div class="entry-category-measles entry-category-vaccination entry-author-crof entry-type-post entry" id="entry-6a00d83451bd6d69e2019aff6d6e47970d">
			<h3 class="entry-header">US: Most cases of measles spread due to philosophical objections</h3>
	
	<div class="entry-content">
		<div class="entry-body">
			I&#39;m now based in a lovely Stockholm hotel, and it&#39;s raining hard. Via UPI.com:&#0160;<a href="http://www.upi.com/Health_News/2013/09/15/Virtually-all-US-measles-cases-in-2013-imported-unvaccinated/UPI-45651379224111/" title="Most U.S. cases of measles spread due to philosophical objections - UPI.com">Most U.S. cases of measles spread due to philosophical objections</a>. Excerpt:
<blockquote cite="http://www.upi.com/Health_News/2013/09/15/Virtually-all-US-measles-cases-in-2013-imported-unvaccinated/UPI-45651379224111/">The United States experienced a higher than normal number of measles cases this year -- mostly due to philosophical objections to vaccines, researchers say.&#0160;
</blockquote>
<blockquote cite="http://www.upi.com/Health_News/2013/09/15/Virtually-all-US-measles-cases-in-2013-imported-unvaccinated/UPI-45651379224111/">Measles elimination -- absence of year-round transmission -- was declared in 2000 in the United States, but measles is imported into the country from countries where it is still common.&#0160;
</blockquote>
<blockquote cite="http://www.upi.com/Health_News/2013/09/15/Virtually-all-US-measles-cases-in-2013-imported-unvaccinated/UPI-45651379224111/">Eight outbreaks and 159 cases of measles were reported to the U.S. Centers for Disease Control between Jan. 1 and Aug. 24. An outbreak in New York City was the largest U.S. outbreak since 1996 at 58 cases, the CDC said.&#0160;
</blockquote>
<blockquote cite="http://www.upi.com/Health_News/2013/09/15/Virtually-all-US-measles-cases-in-2013-imported-unvaccinated/UPI-45651379224111/">Ninety-nine percent of the measles cases were from people from other countries or from Americans who visited other countries, while 82 percent were unvaccinated and 9 percent had unknown vaccination status, the report said.&#0160;
</blockquote>
<blockquote cite="http://www.upi.com/Health_News/2013/09/15/Virtually-all-US-measles-cases-in-2013-imported-unvaccinated/UPI-45651379224111/">Among U.S. residents who were unvaccinated, 79 percent said they had philosophical objection to vaccination.</blockquote>
&quot;Philosophical objections&quot; seems an odd term to use when the thinkers involved would likely be dead (or never even conceived) without decades of vaccination development and application. A term like &quot;solipsistic objection&quot; might be more appropriate.
		</div>
