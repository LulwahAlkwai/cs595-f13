   The 2 Year Turn Around Progress for New CEO Rory Read
   There is a pattern to a successful turn around and given it takes
   between 5 and 7 years to pull one of these off, you have to look for a
   pattern match in the early years. Rory Read is approaching his 2 year
   anniversary had has built a powerful team, put in place a strategy
   that, on its face, should allow them to move around Intel, and has
   begun executing on that strategy at both the client and the server ends
   of the market. So far this is actually a textbook turn around which
   just showcases how difficult it is to do one of these things. I talk
