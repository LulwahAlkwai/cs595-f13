   By Amanda Wills2013-09-16 15:17:50 UTC

   This is a developing story. We will continue to update this article as
   more information becomes available.

   UPDATE - 11:22 p.m. ET: A shooting rampage at the Washington Navy Yard
   on Monday has left 13 people dead, including the gunman, according to
   [48]multiple [49]reports.
   The [50]FBI identified the suspected shooter as Aaron Alexis, 34, whose
   last-known address was in Fort Worth, Texas.

   Police are on the hunt for a suspect or suspects who shot 10 people at
   the Washington Navy Yard, according to [51]reports. The U.S. Navy said
   three shots were fired around 8:20 a.m. Monday at the Naval Sea Systems
   Command Headquarters building.

   As more information becomes available, Mashable will continue to update
   this article with credible sources to follow online about this breaking
   news event.
