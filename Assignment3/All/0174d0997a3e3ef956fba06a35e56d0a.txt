   <h1 class="title" id="page-title">UW CSE News</h1>

<div class="post" id="post-9829">
<h2 class="storytitle"><a href="http://news.cs.washington.edu/2013/04/22/david-notkin-1955-2013/" rel="bookmark" title="Permalink to David Notkin, 1955-2013">David Notkin, 1955-2013</a></h2>

<div class="storycontent">
<p><a href="http://news.cs.washington.edu/2013/02/01/honoring-david-notkin/" target="_blank"><img class="alignright size-medium wp-image-9520" style="margin: 5px; border: 0px none;" alt="dn.for_.fellowship" src="http://news.cs.washington.edu/wp-content/uploads/2013/02/dn.for_.fellowship1-300x200.jpg" width="300" height="200" /></a>Our dear friend and colleague David Notkin passed away at home at 3:30 a.m. on April 22 2013 following a long battle with cancer.  Our hearts go out to David&#8217;s wife Cathy, his children Emma and Akiva, his sister Debbie, and all who knew him and loved him.</p>
<p>Information may be found on David&#8217;s CaringBridge page <a title="David Notkin's CaringBridge page" href="http://www.caringbridge.org/visit/davidnotkin" target="_blank">here</a>.</p>
<p>In February, hundreds of David&#8217;s friends honored him at <a title="Notkinfest post" href="http://news.cs.washington.edu/2013/02/01/honoring-david-notkin/" target="_blank">Notkinfest</a> &#8211; a tribute to his extraordinary personal and professional contributions &#8211; where we announced the establishment of the <a title="Notkin Fellowship" href="http://www.cs.washington.edu/fellowship/notkin/" target="_blank">David Notkin Endowed Graduate Fellowship in Computer Science &amp; Engineering</a> to permanently recognize his dedication to graduate education.</p>
<p>David&#8217;s long list of professional accolades includes, most recently, the <a title="Habermann Award post" href="http://news.cs.washington.edu/2013/02/26/david-notkin-wins-computing-research-association-a-nico-habermann-award/" target="_blank">2013 A. Nico Habermann Award</a> from the Computing Research Association, and the <a title="SIGSOFT Award post" href="http://news.cs.washington.edu/2013/02/20/uw-cses-david-notkin-wins-acm-sigsoft-outstanding-research-award/" target="_blank">2013 ACM SIGSOFT Outstanding Research Award</a> from the Association for Computing Machinery&#8217;s special interest group on software engineering.  He received the <a title="Distinguished Graduate Mentor Award" href="http://www.washington.edu/alumni/columns/june00/best2000notkin.html" target="_blank">University of Washington Distinguished Graduate Mentor Award</a> in 2000.</p>
<p>A few of the many posts honoring David:</p>
<ul>
<li><a title="CCC post" href="http://www.cccblog.org/2013/04/22/in-memoriam-david-notkin/" target="_blank">Computing Community Consortium</a></li>
<li><a title="NCWIT post" href="http://www.ncwit.org/blog/memoriam-david-notkin" target="_blank">National Center for Women &amp; Information Technology</a></li>
<li><a title="UW post" href="http://www.washington.edu/news/2013/04/22/professor-of-computer-science-and-engineering-honored-through-uw-scholarship-fund/" target="_blank">University of Washington</a></li>
<li><em><a title="CACM post" href="http://cacm.acm.org/news/163695-software-engineer-david-notkin-1955-2013/fulltext" target="_blank">Communications of the ACM</a></em></li>
<li><a title="Seattle Times on Notkin" href="http://seattletimes.com/html/localnews/2020853815_jdlcolumn25xml.html" target="_blank"><em>Seattle Times</em>: &#8220;Computing mensch had special way with people&#8221;</a> (<a title="Jerry Large" href="http://lazowska.cs.washington.edu/Jerry.on.David.pdf" target="_blank">pdf</a>) (<a title="Jerry Large" href="http://lazowska.cs.washington.edu/Jerry.on.David.jpg" target="_blank">jpg</a>)</li>
<li><a title="Brown article on DN" href="http://cs.brown.edu/news/2013/04/24/david-notkin-77-1955-2013/" target="_blank">Brown University (David&#8217;s undergraduate alma mater)</a></li>

<li><a title="News post on NCWIT David Notkin Mentorship Award" href="http://news.cs.washington.edu/2013/05/20/ncwit-establishes-david-notkin-mentorship-award/" target="_blank">NCWIT establishes the David Notkin Mentorship Award</a></li>
<li><a title="ACM/CRA video tribute to David" href="http://news.cs.washington.edu/2013/06/18/cra-and-acm-honor-david-notkin/" target="_blank">ACM/CRA video tribute to David shown at 2013 ACM Awards Banquet</a></li>
<li><a title="MSB" href="http://www.cs.washington.edu/public_files/publications/msb/msb23.1.pdf" target="_blank">Spring 2013 <em>Most Significant Bits</em> (UW CSE alumni magazine)</a></li>
