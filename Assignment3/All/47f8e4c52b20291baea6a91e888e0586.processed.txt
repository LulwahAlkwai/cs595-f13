A Century of Erdo"s

   [5][about_erdos.jpg]
   The great Hungarian combinatorialist [6]Paul Erdo"s was born one
   hundred years ago today. The [7]big celebration will happen in Budapest
   in July.
   It's hard to say more about Erdo"s than I've already said in this blog
   so let's recap some of those highlights.
     * [8]What is your Erdo"s number?
     * [9]The Erdo"s-de Bruijn Theorem
     * [10]The Erdo"s distance problem solved
     * [11]Erdo"s-Szekeres monotone sequence theorem
     * [12]Erdo"s-Rado sunflower theorem
     * [13]Erdo"s-Turan conjecture
     * [14]Erdo"s and the "stupid question"
     * [15]Solving math problems for money
     * [16]Sum-Product Theorems
     * [17]Sauer's Lemma (which may or may not have been first conjectured
       by Erdo"s)

   Shiva also [18]celebrates the centenary of Erdo"s and I second his
   suggestion to check out [19]The Man Who Loved Only Numbers, Paul
   Hoffman's great biography about his life.
