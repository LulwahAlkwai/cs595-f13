<p>The Internet might be drunk.</p>
<p>And after you watch this mashup of <a href="http://mashable.com/category/miley-cyrus/">Miley Cyrus</a> with Nicolas Cage, you'll probably agree. </p>

It's as weird as it sounds — and don't expect to shake it from your brain anytime soon. </p>
