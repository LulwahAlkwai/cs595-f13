<section class='article-content'>

<p>Amazon Instant Video has finally been updated with <a href="http://appadvice.com/appnn/2013/06/the-amazon-instant-video-app-still-doesnt-offer-full-airplay-support" target="_blank">full AirPlay support</a>. As a result, you can now use the app to watch titles from Prime Instant Video and the Amazon Instant Video store right on your Apple TV via AirPlay.</p>
<p>In addition to AirPlay support, another new feature is integration with IMDb, which Amazon also owns. This lets you see IMDb cast and director information, ratings, goofs, trivia and quotes right within Amazon Instant Video. This harks back to the <a href="http://getapp.cc/app/342792525" target="_blank">IMDb</a> iOS app’s<a href="http://appadvice.com/appnn/2013/02/imdb-movies-tv-updated-with-amazon-instant-video-integration-and-more" target="_blank"> update</a> seven months ago, which brought Amazon Instant Video integration.</p>
<p>The latest update to Amazon Instant Video also includes the following enhancements:</p>
<ul>
<li><p>Read customer reviews</p></li>
<li><p>Find other videos featuring cast or director</p></li>
<li><p>Customers who watched this also watched …</p></li>
<li><p>Concurrent downloads</p></li>
<li><p>Improved offline experience</p></li>
<li><p>Faster and more responsive interface</p></li>
<li><p>Bug fixes and stability improvements</p></li>
</ul>
<p>The new version of Amazon Instant Video is <a href="http://getapp.cc/app/545519333" target="_blank">available now </a>in the App Store for free. The app is compatible with iPhone, iPod touch and iPad running iOS 5 or later.</p>
<p>Amazon Instant Video was <a href="http://appadvice.com/appnn/2012/08/amazon-launches-instant-video-app-for-ipad-stream-content-download-for-offline-viewing" target="_blank">released</a> intially for iPad in August last year. It was made compatible for iPhone and iPod touch through its <a href="http://appadvice.com/appnn/2012/12/amazon-instant-video-now-available-on-iphoneipod-touch" target="_blank">universal update</a> the following December.</p>
