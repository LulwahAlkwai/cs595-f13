Event Details

   About the Event:

   To grow a powerful professional network, you need connections that
   matter. CTU's "Power of In" is a professional networking event that
   will demonstrate what the power of networking and mentorship can do for
   your career. The event features a keynote speech from New York Times
   bestselling author and leadership expert Charlene Li, and tips and
   trends workshops from LinkedIn.

   Aside from mingling with real-world experts and peers, enjoy specialty,
   custom-made libations, hors d'oeuvres, free giveaways and more!

   About Charlene Li:

   Charlene Li is founder of Altimeter Group and the author of the New
   York Times bestseller, Open Leadership. She is also the coauthor of the
   critically acclaimed, bestselling book Groundswell, which was named one
   of the best business books in 2008.

   Charlene is one of the foremost experts on social media and
   technologies, and a consultant and independent thought leader on
   leadership, strategy, social technologies, interactive media and
   marketing. She was named one of the 100 most creative people in
   business by Fast Company in 2010 and one of the most influential women
   in technology 2009.

   Charlene is frequently quoted by leading media channels such as The
   Wall Street Journal, New York Times, USA Today, Reuters, and The
   Associated Press. She has appeared on 60 Minutes, The McNeil NewsHour,
   ABC News, CNN, and CNBC.

   For more on Charlene, visit [14]http://www.charleneli.com/.

   FAQs

   What's the easiest way to get to the event?
   For directions to Chicago Illuminating, please click [15]here.

   Is there nearby parking?
   Free valet parking available.

   Is public transportation an option?
   Yes, CTA bus and train stops are within walking distance of Chicago
   Illuminating Company.

   What is the dress code?
   Dress to impress! Business casual is recommended.

   Why should I attend?
   You get the opportunity to mix and mingle with CTU staff, alumni and
   students in a fun and interactive environment, allowing you to forge
   new relationships that may positively impact your career.

   Who can I see at the event?
   Current students, alumni, university leaders and faculty members who
   are located in the Chicago area and currently working in your field.
   Additionally, you'll have the chance to connect directly with keynote
   speaker and bestselling author, Charlene Li.

   What can I expect?
   Aside from mingling with local students, alumni, university leaders and
   faculty members, you'll enjoy a keynote speech from bestselling author
   Charlene Li and tips on how to leverage your online connections and
   strengthen your personal brand from experts at LinkedIn. Additionally
   custom-made libations, hors d'ouevres, desserts and giveaways will be
   provided.

   Will there be food?
    Though hors d'ouevres and some dessert options will be available for
   light snacking, it's recommended that you eat beforehand.

   Will there be alcohol?
    Mocktail Hour will consist of specialty, alcohol-free libations.

   What time should I arrive?
   Doors open at 5:30pm, while Mocktail Hour and LinkedIn workshops kick
   off at 6pm. If you're running late, we will still have staff available
   to get you checked in and show where to go.

   Do I need to bring anything?
   Please bring your ID for registration. Social media posting is also
   highly encouraged so don't forget your smart phone!

   Where can I contact the organizer with any questions?
   You can visit our organizer page, or contact CTU at
   social@ctuonline.edu.

   Can I bring a guest?
   Guest passes are limited. For availability, please contact CTU at
   social@ctuonline.edu.
