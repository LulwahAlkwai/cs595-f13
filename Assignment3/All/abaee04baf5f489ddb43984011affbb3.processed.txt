Thank you for attending CEDIA EXPO.

With more than 470 exhibitors and 17,900 attendees from 84 countries, CEDIA
EXPO 2013 was a success!

   The show saw positive gains across the board including: 6% growth in
   professional and overall attendance, 20% growth in new exhibitor
   participation, 50% growth in first-time attendees, and a 50% CEDIA
   Training participation increase!
   Let's keep the momentum going in 2014. We'll be back in Denver
   September 10-13, 2014 with the tradeshow floor open September 11-13,
   2014.

   Although CEDIA EXPO 2013 was not impacted by the recent floods in
   Colorado, many of our members and fellow industry professionals have
   been impacted. If you'd like to help, please visit
   [25]HelpColoradoNow.com.
