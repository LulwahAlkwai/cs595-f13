          <div class="date-posts">

<h3 class='post-title entry-title' itemprop='name'>
GDAT Class October 14-18, 2013
</h3>
<div class='post-header'>
<div class='post-header-line-1'></div>
</div>
<div class='post-body entry-content' id='post-body-6538721154699491248' itemprop='articleBody'>
This is your <i>fast track</i> to enterprise performance and capacity management with an emphasis on applying <a href="http://www.r-project.org">R statistical tools</a> to your performance data.
<p>
<a href="https://www.eventbrite.com/event/8048845313?ref=elink" target="_blank"">Early-bird discounts</a> are available for the Level III 
<a href="http://www.perfdynamics.com/Classes/Outlines/gdata.html">Guerrilla Data Analysis Techniques</a> class Oct 14&mdash;18.
<p>
<center><a href="http://www.perfdynamics.com/Classes/schedule.html"><img alt="Entrance Larkspur Landing hotel Pleasanton California" height="173" src="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/_MPdbS9EPh5Y/TFSvF5F97JI/AAAAAAAAArs/XkqSZlk6Zy4/s320/Larkspur-Pleasanton.jpg" width="365" /></a></center>
<p>
As usual, all classes are held at our lovely <a href="http://www.larkspurhotels.com/larkspur-landing/pleasanton">Larkspur Landing</a> location in Pleasanton. Attendees should bring their <span style="font-weight: bold;">laptops</span> to the class  as course materials are provided on a flash drive. Larkspur Landing also provides free <span style="font-weight: bold;">wi-fi</span> Internet in their residence-style rooms as well as the training room.
<p>
Some of the topics covered include:
<ul>
<li> Introduction to using R
<li> How to Detect Bad Data
<li> Measurement Errors and Analysis
<li> Multivariate Linear and Nonlinear Regression
<li> Quantitative Scalability Analysis
<li> Statistical forecasting techniques for CaP
<li> Machine learning algorithms applied to CaP Data
<li> Wild Not Mild Data: SQL access, web traffic, data recovery
<li> Taming the Data Torrent with PCA and PerfViz techniques
<li> Bayesian analysis for the bewildered (<b>New!</b>)
</ul>
<p>
Before <a href="https://www.eventbrite.com/event/8048845313?ref=elink" target="_blank">registering online</a>, take a look at what former students have said about their <a href="http://www.perfdynamics.com/Classes/comments.html">Guerrilla experience</a>.
