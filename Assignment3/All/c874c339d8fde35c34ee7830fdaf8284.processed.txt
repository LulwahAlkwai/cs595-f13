AMC Greenlights 'Breaking Bad' Spinoff That Will Focus on Saul Goodman

   10.0k
   Shares
   [44]Share
   [45]Tweet
   [46]Share

   Breaking-bad-saul-goodman
   [47]Bah-sq
   By Brian Anthony Hernandez2013-09-11 20:54:01 UTC

   After the fifth and final season of [48]Breaking Bad ends this month,
   AMC's hit drama will live on through a spinoff about criminal lawyer
   Saul Goodman. Tentatively titled Better Call Saul, the series will
   serve as a prequel to Breaking Bad's five seasons.

   [49]AMC and Sony Pictures Television revealed Wednesday they signed a
   licensing deal for the spinoff, adding in a joint announcement that the
   new series "will focus on the evolution of the popular Saul Goodman
   character before he ever became Walter White's lawyer."

   See also: [50]Tread Lightly Through These 17 'Breaking Bad' Inspired
   Vines

   Actor Bob Odenkirk plays Saul Goodman. No further details were
   divulged, but it appears a AMC-driven "Better Call Saul" [51]website --
   full of client testimonials -- already exists.

   Are you looking forward to the Breaking Bad spinoff? Let us know in the
   comments below.
