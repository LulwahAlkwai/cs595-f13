									<div class="post_meta">
										The complexity of a string <i>x</i> of length <i>n</i> in this game is
										the minimum number of states of a nondeterministic finite state automaton, accepting <i>x</i>,
										that accepts along only one path of length <i>n</i>. It was studied in
										<a href="http://math.hawaii.edu/graduate-alumni/kkhyde/">Kayleigh Hyde's MA thesis</a>.
