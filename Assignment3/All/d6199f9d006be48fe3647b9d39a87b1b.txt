					<div class="entry-meta">
													<span class="meta-prep meta-prep-author">Posted on</span> <a href="http://goodmorninggloucester.wordpress.com/2012/06/07/video-interview-the-summer-retreat-2012-preview-with-lindsay-crouse-and-rick-blue/" title="8:00 am" rel="bookmark"><span class="entry-date">June 7, 2012</span></a>							<span class="by-author"><span class="sep">by</span> <span class="author vcard"><a class="url fn n" href="http://goodmorninggloucester.wordpress.com/author/captjoe06/" title="View all posts by Joey C" rel="author">Joey C</a></span> </span>											</div><!-- .entry-meta -->

					<div class="entry-content">
						<p>click below for the interview</p>
<p><a href="http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=R9e53e2GzkI" target="_blank"><img style="background-image:none;border-bottom:0;border-left:0;margin:0 auto;padding-left:0;padding-right:0;display:block;float:none;border-top:0;border-right:0;padding-top:0;" title="image" border="0" alt="image" src="http://goodmorninggloucester.files.wordpress.com/2012/06/image43.png?w=641&#038;h=391" width="641" height="391" /></a></p>
<p>The eight annual Summer Retreat is just a couple of months away!</p>
<p>For More Info- <a href="http://www.thesummerretreat.org">www.thesummerretreat.org</a> </p>
<p><a href="http://goodmorninggloucester.files.wordpress.com/2012/06/sr_29may_postcard-for-web.jpg"><img title="SR_29may_postcard-for-web" border="0" alt="SR_29may_postcard-for-web" src="http://goodmorninggloucester.files.wordpress.com/2012/06/sr_29may_postcard-for-web_thumb.jpg?w=644&amp;h=418" width="644" height="418" /></a></p>
<p>The Retreat is a rare opportunity for people to take a break from their busy lives and experience something wonderful. It’s a chance to:</p>
<p><b>Rest and revitalize</b> with life-changing happiness teachings from world-class teachers and master practitioners – including the amazing Lama Marut, author of <i>A Spiritual Renegade’s Guide to the Good Life</i>.</p>
<p><b>Enjoy</b> meditation, yoga at all levels and community in the beauty of pastoral New England.</p>
<p><b>Understand</b> how to live in happiness for six days…so you can be happy the rest of your life.</p>
<p>The retreat is led by Lama Marut — an American Buddhist monk whose teachings are both profound and completely engaging. He will make you laugh. He will make you think. He will probably change your life.</p>
<p>You can check out his podcasts – including podcasts from last year’s Summer Retreat – on iTunes.</p>
<p>Thanks to Joey and Good Morning Gloucester for supporting the Summer Retreat!</p>
