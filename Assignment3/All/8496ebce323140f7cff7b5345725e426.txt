<div class="shift-to-hero"><p><iframe allowfullscreen="" frameborder="0" height="360" src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/My2FRPA3Gf8" width="640"></iframe></p></div>
<p>Reminiscent of Sinéad O'Connor's "Nothing Compares 2 U" video, <a href="http://mashable.com/category/miley-cyrus/">Miley Cyrus</a>' music video for power ballad "Wrecking Ball" resonates with beauty shots of the tearful singer. The softer approach is a stark contrast from Cyrus' <a href="http://mashable.com/2013/09/03/miley-cyrus-responds-vmas/" data-crackerjax="#post-slider">performance at the MTV VMAs</a> in which she twerked on Robin Thicke while wearing a large foam finger.</p>
<p>Cyrus licks a sledgehammer and chain of a wrecking ball, at times riding the destructive sphere in the nude. Famed fashion and portrait photographer Terry Richardson directed for "Wrecking Ball," which sits tight at No. 9 on iTunes top singles chart.
