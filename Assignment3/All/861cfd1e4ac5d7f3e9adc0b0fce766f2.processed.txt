Watch Our Live Hangout With U.S. Soccer Star Jozy Altidore

   725
   Shares
   [44]Share
   [45]Tweet
   [46]Share
   What's This?
   [47]Sam,%20sq
   By Sam Laird2013-09-11 17:00:58 UTC

   IFRAME: [48]https://www.youtube.com/embed/TPcD4AkEhmg

   With Tuesday night's qualifying win over Mexico, [49]Jozy Altidore and
   the United States national team are officially headed to Brazil for the
   2014 World Cup. But first, some more pressing business for the team's
   star striker: a live Google+ Hangout On Air with Mashable on Wednesday
   at 2 p.m. ET.

   Altidore was forced to sit during Tuesday's match after drawing a
   yellow card against Costa Rica last week, but he set a national team
   record this summer by scoring in five straight matches. He joins us
   today from the UK, where he plays club [50]soccer for Sunderland in the
   English Premier League. We'll talk a little bit about Altidore's own
   digital habits, but we're leaving most of this show up to you, the fans
   on social media.

   Submit your questions for Altidore using the hashtag #AskJozy on the
   [51]Google+ event page, Facebook, Twitter or in the comments section of
   this post. It's all fair game: Altidore's favorite countries to visit,
   his biggest soccer challenges and his most memorable on-field moments.

   Then tune in here at 2 p.m. ET for the conversation. You can even
   return later on to watch a replay. You've got to do something to kill
   all the time until the World Cup, right?

   What questions do you have for Altidore? Let us know in the comments.
