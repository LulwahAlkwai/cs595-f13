   Video
   Review of Open Innovation Forum 2012 Events

   The global discussions platform -- the Open Innovations Forum -- is
   devoted to the latest technologies and prospects of international
   cooperation in the field of innovations. The event is focused on
   forecasting of technological trends, determination of the role of an
   innovative ecosystem in global world processes and formation of the
   Russian and international agendas of innovative development. The forum
   is held under the aegis of the Government of the Russian Federation and
   with the assistance of the Government of Moscow, as well as the Russian
   institutes of development: OJSC ROSNANO, OJSC RVK, Skolkovo fund, the
   State Corporation Bank for Development and Foreign Affairs
   (Vnesheconombank), the Fund of Assistance to the Development of the
   Small Forms of Enterprises in the Scientific-and-Technical Sphere, ANO
   "Agency of strategic initiatives for promotion of new projects",
   Chamber of commerce and industry of the Russian Federation. The
   intellectual producer of the Open Innovations 2013 Forum is MIT
   Technology Review. The Chairman of the Organizing Committee of the
   Forum is Vladislav Surkov, Deputy Prime Minister of the Russian
   Federation -- Chief of the Government Staff.
