12th International Conference on Entertainment Computing

Call for Papers

   The IFIP International Conference on Entertainment Computing explores
   the application of computational technology to entertainment. The
   conference brings together practitioners and researchers interested in
   the art and design of entertainment computing applications. ICEC 2013
   welcomes submissions on the design, engineering, application and theory
   of entertainment technology. We solicit paper, poster and demonstration
   submissions, as well as proposals for tutorials and workshops.

   Papers will be published by Springer as a LNCS and should be in the
   required format
   ([17]http://www.springer.com/computer/lncs?SGWID=0-164-6-793341-0).
   They will also be published in the ACM Digital Library..

Submission Types and Deadlines

   28 April 2013
     * Tutorial/Workshop submissions (maximum length of 4 pages)

   07 May 2013
     * Full Technical Papers (maximum length of 12 pages)
     * Short Technical Papers (maximum length of 6 pages)
     * Interactive Art Installation Proposals (maximum length of 2 pages
       plus companion multimedia material)

   11 June 2013
     * Full, Short Technical Papers and Interactive Art Installation
       Proposals notifications

   18 June 2013
     * Poster Papers (maximum length of 4 pages)
     * Demonstration Papers (maximum length of 4 pages)
     * Industrial Papers (maximum length of 4 pages)
     * Doctoral Consortium Papers (maximum length of 4 pages)

   8 July 2013
     * Poster, Demonstration, Industrial Papers and Doctoral Consortium
       notifications

   13 July 2013
     * Camera-ready submission

   Submissions shall be made through EasyChair:
   [18]https://www.easychair.org/conferences/?conf=icec20130.
