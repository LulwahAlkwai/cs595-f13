[23]David Notkin, 1955-2013

   [24]dn.for_.fellowship Our dear friend and colleague David Notkin
   passed away at home at 3:30 a.m. on April 22 2013 following a long
   battle with cancer.  Our hearts go out to David's wife Cathy, his
   children Emma and Akiva, his sister Debbie, and all who knew him and
   loved him.

   Information may be found on David's CaringBridge page [25]here.

   In February, hundreds of David's friends honored him at [26]Notkinfest
   - a tribute to his extraordinary personal and professional
   contributions - where we announced the establishment of the [27]David
   Notkin Endowed Graduate Fellowship in Computer Science & Engineering to
   permanently recognize his dedication to graduate education.

   David's long list of professional accolades includes, most recently,
   the [28]2013 A. Nico Habermann Award from the Computing Research
   Association, and the [29]2013 ACM SIGSOFT Outstanding Research Award
   from the Association for Computing Machinery's special interest group
   on software engineering.  He received the [30]University of Washington
   Distinguished Graduate Mentor Award in 2000.
