<section class='article-content'>

<p><a href="http://mashable.com/2013/09/10/infinity-blade-iii/" data-crackerjax="#post-slider">Infinity Blade III</a>, the game used to showcase the iPhone 5S' capabilities during Apple's recent iPhone <a href="http://mashable.com/2013/09/10/apple-unveils-iphone-5s/" data-crackerjax="#post-slider">event</a>, is now available for purchase. </p>
<p>The release comes hours before the scheduled release of iOS 7, which should become available sometime today. </p>
<p>The game takes full advantage of iPhone 5S' A7 64-bit processor — the first game to do so. </p>
<div class="see-also"><p>See also: <a href="http://mashable.com/2013/09/10/iphone-5s-hands-on/" data-crackerjax="#post-slider">iPhone 5S Hands On: All Change Under the Hood</a></p></div>
<p>You can get the Infinity Blade III for the iPhone and iPad for $6.99 in <a href="https://itunes.apple.com/app/id610003290?mt=8" target="_blank">Apple's App Store.</a> </p>
