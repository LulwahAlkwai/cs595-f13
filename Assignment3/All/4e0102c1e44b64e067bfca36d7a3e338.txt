<p>Who needs Starbucks when you can have your own personal caffeine marker at ALL times? Yes, you heard me right. Say hello, world, to the portable espresso machine. Just in time for finals.</p>
<p>Ain&#8217;t nobody got time to crash from a coffee high, and<strong> The Nomad Espresso Machine</strong> knows that. No batteries, electricity or high-pressure gas cartridges required. Oh, did we mention it&#8217;s green, too? Like, beyond just the color.</p>
<p>It&#8217;s hand operated too, so all you need to do is pack ground coffee, add hot water and push. Presto! Insta &#8220;hand-crafted&#8221; espresso.</p>
<p><small><em>Nomad <strong>$165</strong> @<a href="http://www.kickstarter.com/projects/uniterra-nomad/nomadtm-the-go-everywhere-espresso-machinetm" target="_blank">Kickstarter</a></em></small></p>
