            <td colspan="3"><blockquote>
              <p>Over the last decades a wide gulf has emerged between the number of postdoctoral fellowship positions and the availability of academic faculty positions. &nbsp;&nbsp;For many postdocs industry offers a promising alternative. &nbsp;</p>
              <p>Careers in industry can be both exciting and rewarding, offering real world application of cutting edge science and making a beneficial impact upon people&#8217;s lives. &nbsp;</p>
              <ul>
                <li>What skills do postdoctoral scientists need to enter the corporate workforce? &nbsp;</li>
                <li>Why isn&#8217;t a PhD always sufficient, especially to work for companies where science is the fundamental currency of the business? &nbsp;&nbsp;</li>
              </ul>
              <p>The answers to these questions are complex and require a detailed understanding of how the corporate world operates, and how it is fundamentally different from the academy. &nbsp;&nbsp;This panel will discuss the career landscape facing postdocs interested in moving into the life sciences industry.            </p>
            </blockquote>            </td>
