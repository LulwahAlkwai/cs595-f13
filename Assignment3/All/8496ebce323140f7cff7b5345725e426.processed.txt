   By Brian Anthony Hernandez2013-09-09 16:38:45 UTC

   IFRAME: [48]https://www.youtube.com/embed/My2FRPA3Gf8

   Reminiscent of Sinéad O'Connor's "Nothing Compares 2 U" video,
   [49]Miley Cyrus' music video for power ballad "Wrecking Ball" resonates
   with beauty shots of the tearful singer. The softer approach is a stark
   contrast from Cyrus' [50]performance at the MTV VMAs in which she
   twerked on Robin Thicke while wearing a large foam finger.

   Cyrus licks a sledgehammer and chain of a wrecking ball, at times
   riding the destructive sphere in the nude. Famed fashion and portrait
   photographer Terry Richardson directed for "Wrecking Ball," which sits
   tight at No. 9 on iTunes top singles chart. Aside from the video, Cyrus
   on Monday also was announced as Saturday Night Live's Oct. 5 host and
   performer.

   See also: [51]Miley Delivers 'Bangerz' Track List After 'Wrecking Ball'
   Breaks Record

   Cyrus is encouraging fans online to help her [53]reclaim the Vevo
   record for most video views in 24 hours. She earned that record for "We
   Can't Stop" in June before relinquishing the honor to One Direction's
   "[54]Best Song Ever." Cyrus will release the track list for her
   forthcoming album, Bangerz, if her Smilers fan base breaks the record.

     Lets beat the vevo record & ill release my track list for
     [55]#bangerz

   on Twitter with [59]4.5 million mentions during the initial broadcast.
