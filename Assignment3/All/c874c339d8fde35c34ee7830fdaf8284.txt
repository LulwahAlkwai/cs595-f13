<p>After the fifth and final season of <a href="http://mashable.com/category/breaking-bad/"><em>Breaking Bad</em></a> ends this month, AMC's hit drama will live on through a spinoff about criminal lawyer Saul Goodman. Tentatively titled <em>Better Call Saul</em>, the series will serve as a prequel to <em>Breaking Bad</em>'s five seasons. </p>
<p><a href="http://mashable.com/category/amc/">AMC</a> and Sony Pictures Television revealed Wednesday they signed a licensing deal for the spinoff, adding in a joint announcement that the new series "will focus on the evolution of the popular Saul Goodman character before he ever became Walter White’s lawyer."</p>
<div class="see-also"><p>See also: <a href="http://mashable.com/2013/08/25/breaking-bad-vine/" data-crackerjax="#post-slider">Tread Lightly Through These 17 'Breaking Bad' Inspired Vines</a></p></div>
<p>Actor Bob Odenkirk plays Saul Goodman. No further details were divulged, but it appears a AMC-driven "Better Call Saul" <a href="http://www.bettercallsaul.com/" target="_blank">website</a> — full of client testimonials — already exists.</p>
<p>Are you looking forward to the <em>Breaking Bad</em> spinoff? Let us know in the comments below.</p>
