<div class="shift-to-hero"><p><iframe allowfullscreen="" frameborder="0" height="360" src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/TPcD4AkEhmg" width="640"></iframe></p></div>
<p>With Tuesday night's qualifying win over Mexico, <a href="http://mashable.com/2012/10/12/jozy-altidore-official-site/" data-crackerjax="#post-slider">Jozy Altidore</a> and the United States national team are officially headed to Brazil for the 2014 World Cup. But first, some more pressing business for the team's star striker: a live Google+ Hangout On Air with <em>Mashable</em> on Wednesday at 2 p.m. ET. </p>
<p>Altidore was forced to sit during Tuesday's match after drawing a yellow card against Costa Rica last week, but he set a national team record this summer by scoring in five straight matches. He joins us today from the UK, where he plays club <a href="http://mashable.com/category/soccer/">soccer</a> for Sunderland in the English Premier League. We'll talk a little bit about Altidore's own digital habits, but we're leaving most of this show up to you, the fans on social media. </p>
<p>Submit your questions for Altidore using the hashtag #AskJozy on the <a href="https://plus.google.com/events/cirdec97h8av15iuf023t9hrrdo" target="_blank">Google+ event page</a>, Facebook, Twitter or in the comments section of this post.  It's all fair game: Altidore's favorite countries to visit, his biggest soccer challenges and his most memorable on-field moments.</p>
<p>Then tune in here at 2 p.m. ET for the conversation. You can even return later on to watch a replay. You've got to do <em>something</em> to kill all the time until the World Cup, right?  </p>
<p>What questions do you have for Altidore? Let us know in the comments. </p>
