			<h3>The Power of Temboo: connect the Yún to 100+ APIs</h3>
			<div class="info">
				<span class="author">Zoe Romano</span> &mdash; <span class="date">September 11th, 2013</span>
			</div>
			
			
			<div class="entry">
				<p><a href="http://blog.arduino.cc/wp-content/uploads/2013/09/1-Arduino-Temboo-schema.png"><img class="alignnone  wp-image-8032" title="1 - Arduino Temboo schema" alt="1 - Arduino Temboo schema" src="http://blog.arduino.cc/wp-content/uploads/2013/09/1-Arduino-Temboo-schema-1024x453.png" width="614" height="272" /></a></p>
<p><em>(Guest post by Temboo Team)</em></p>
<p>Ever wish your Arduino could respond to the weather on the other side of the world? Or send you an email to let you know what it&#8217;s up to? Upload stuff to your Dropbox account? Or detect if you&#8217;re at risk of exposure to toxic chemicals?</p>
<p>Now it can. The <a title="http://arduino.cc/en/Main/ArduinoYUN" href="http://arduino.cc/en/Main/ArduinoYUN">Arduino Yún</a> can easily grab all sorts of data and interact with tons of web-based services like Fitbit, Facebook, and Google because every Yún comes loaded with the power of <a title="Temboo" href="http://www.temboo.com">Temboo</a>. Using the Yún&#8217;s built in wi-fI capabilities, Temboo makes it easy for the Yún to connect to over 100 Application Programming Interfaces (APIs).</p>
<p>Don&#8217;t know what an <a title="API on wikipedia" href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Application_programming_interface">API</a> is or how to use one? Don&#8217;t worry. You don&#8217;t need to. With Temboo you can start using APIs with your Yún in minutes. We&#8217;ve standardized how to program with them and made sure they play nice with Arduino. <span id="more-8031"></span></p>
<p><strong>So how do you get your Yún to Temboo?</strong></p>
<p>That&#8217;s easy too. When you&#8217;re setting up your Arduino account, you can quickly register for a free Temboo account right from your Arduino profile page. All you need to do for that is pick a Temboo username, and we&#8217;ll automatically email you your Temboo account details along with some ideas on where to begin. You should definitely check out the dedicated <a title="Arduino on temboo" href="https://www.temboo.com/arduino">Arduino section</a> of our website. We&#8217;ve created several how-to examples to get you Tweeting, texting, and more from your Yún in minutes. We&#8217;ve even put some examples right into the Arduino IDE (File-&gt;Examples&gt;Bridge-&gt;Temboo).</p>
<p><a href="http://blog.arduino.cc/wp-content/uploads/2013/09/2-Temboos-Arduino-page.png"><img class="alignnone  wp-image-8033" title="Temboo's special page for the Arduino Yún." alt="Temboo's special page for the Arduino Yún." src="http://blog.arduino.cc/wp-content/uploads/2013/09/2-Temboos-Arduino-page.png" width="529" height="517" /></a></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>Click on the image below to see one of the Temboo examples on Arduino IDE</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><a href="http://blog.arduino.cc/wp-content/uploads/2013/09/3-Arduino-IDE-example.png"><img class="alignnone  wp-image-8034" title="One of the Temboo examples in the Arduino IDE." alt="One of the Temboo examples in the Arduino IDE." src="http://blog.arduino.cc/wp-content/uploads/2013/09/3-Arduino-IDE-example-1024x640.png" width="491" height="307" /></a></p>
<p><strong>What&#8217;s under the hood?</strong></p>
<p>Temboo has written a special Python program on the Linino processor that interacts with the Temboo platform, sending and receiving data. A C++ library we created for the Arduino processor allows makers to communicate with the Linino-side Temboo program in a powerful yet user-friendly way. Sketch code for interacting with any Temboo process is generated live on our site for copying into the Arduino IDE. We&#8217;ve even developed a unique way for users to select the particular data they need from APIs and web-based resources, filtering out extraneous information and reducing the load on the board. For all the power that Temboo provides, our technology leaves a remarkably light footprint.</p>
<p><a href="http://blog.arduino.cc/wp-content/uploads/2013/09/4-Arduino-sketch-code.png"><img class="alignnone  wp-image-8035" title="The Temboo website generates sketch code for the Arduino Yún. Above: Create an event on a Google Calendar." alt="The Temboo website generates sketch code for the Arduino Yún. Above: Create an event on a Google Calendar." src="http://blog.arduino.cc/wp-content/uploads/2013/09/4-Arduino-sketch-code.png" width="484" height="463" /></a></p>
<p>We can&#8217;t even begin to imagine all the cool stuff that makers will do with the Yún. We&#8217;ll be adding more Arduino examples to our site as we continue to brainstorm, but we&#8217;d love to feature your ideas and projects as well. Shoot us an email at <em>hey [at] temboo.com</em> or tweet at us at <em>@Temboo</em> to tell us about what you&#8217;re working on or to ask any questions.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
