   The O'Reilly Reader Review Program is a great way for you to get first
   access to new ebooks and videos focused on leading-edge software,
   technology, and social media trends. What's more, you'll have your
   expert opinions heard as a reviewer.

With the Reader Review Program you can:

     * Get FREE access to new ebooks and videos.
     * Receive 1 free ebook or video per month.
     * Post short reviews-between 250 to 500 words-you can even review our
       products on amazon.com!
     * Post your review on amazon.com, oreilly.com, your own website, or
       blog. Reviews can be made on other review sites, such as
       barnesandnoble.com or goodreads.com.
     * Have your site linked on oreilly.com (good for SEO).
     * Have your site linked in our monthly Reader Review Newsletter.
     * Suggest another product if you don't find an available product you
       like.
     * [24]Read more about the program >

    1. Sign up for a [25]free reader review account.
    2. Select the ebook or video you want to read from our [26]list of
       available products. We'll send you the ebook
       or video for free.
    3. Post your review on a blog, website, or consumer website.
       Amazon.com and oreilly.com included!
