Receive a receipt by email, send it to Evernote

this recipe on IFTTT by clicking here. 


Keep a running list in Evernote of newly added streaming titles on Netflix

I am a daily user of Netflix, but keeping track of the new titles that come 
out is hard.  This recipe adds all new streaming titles right to your 
Evernote account.  Find this recipe on IFTTT by clicking here. 


Create a new checklist / to-do note every morning

by clicking here. 


Send emails with attachments to Evernote

Whenever I get an email with an attachment, it automatically sends the file 
to Evernote.  Find this recipe on IFTTT by clicking here."/>
