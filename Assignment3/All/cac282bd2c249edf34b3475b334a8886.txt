                      <h1 property="dc:title" datatype="" class="node-title">Symposium on Visions of the Theory of Computing</h1>
              
              <div class="field field-name-field-workshop-date field-type-datetime field-label-hidden"><div class="field-items"><div class="field-item even"><span class="date-display-start" property="dc:date" datatype="xsd:dateTime" content="2013-05-29T00:00:00-07:00">May 29</span><span class="date-display-separator"> &ndash; </span><span class="date-display-end" property="dc:date" datatype="xsd:dateTime" content="2013-05-31T00:00:00-07:00">May 31, 2013</span></div></div></div>                                </div><!--/.header-inner-->

  <div class="agenda-link"><a href="/workshops/schedule/82">View schedule & video &raquo;</a></div><div class="field field-name-field-organizers-ref field-type-entityreference field-label-above"><div class="field-label">Organizers:&nbsp;</div><div class="field-items"><div class="field-item even"><a href="/people/richard-karp" class="node node-67 entityreference">Richard Karp</a> (UC Berkeley), <a href="/people/christos-papadimitriou" class="node node-69 entityreference">Christos Papadimitriou</a> (UC Berkeley), <a href="/people/alistair-sinclair" class="node node-68 entityreference">Alistair Sinclair</a> (UC Berkeley)<span class="textformatter-fullstop">.</span></div></div></div><div class="field field-name-body field-type-text-with-summary field-label-hidden"><div class="field-items"><div class="field-item even" property="content:encoded"><h2>To view the link for a video or slides, click on the title of the talk in the <a href="/workshops/schedule/82">schedule</a>.</h2>
<p><br /><span style="line-height: 1.57142857em;">This three-day symposium will bring together distinguished speakers and participants from the Bay Area and all over the world to celebrate both the excitement of fundamental research on the Theory of Computing, and the accomplishments and promise of computational research in effecting progress in other sciences — the two pillars of the Institute's research agenda.</span></p>
<h2><span style="line-height: 1.57142857em;">Note</span></h2>
<p>This symposium immediately precedes <a href="http://theory.stanford.edu/stoc2013/" target="_blank">STOC</a>, which runs June 1–4, 2013 in Palo Alto, California.</p>
<div class="collapse-next">

	<h2>Travel Grants for Graduate Students</h2>
	<p>A limited number of travel grants will be available for current graduate students. To apply, please send an email to <span class="spamspan"><span class="u">simonsevents</span> [at] <span class="d">berkeley [dot] edu</span><span class="h"> (subject: Symposium%20Travel) </span></span> with the subject line "Symposium Travel," your CV, and a short research statement (no more than one page). The deadline is <strong>Wednesday, April 24</strong>.</p>
