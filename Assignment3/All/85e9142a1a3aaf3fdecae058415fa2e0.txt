<section class='article-content'>

<p>A new <a href="http://mashable.com/category/youtube/">YouTube</a> video claims to show the back housing for the rumored gold <a href="http://mashable.com/category/iphone-5s/">iPhone 5S</a>, providing some clues about what Apple might release next.</p>
<p><a href="https://twitter.com/keaton" target="_blank">Keaton Keller</a>, of YouTube channel <a href="http://www.youtube.com/TechSmartt" target="_blank">TechSmartt</a>, showed the back housing for what he claims is Apple's next-generation iPhone. The housing is a gold-champagne color, and looks similar in size to the iPhone 5.</p>
<div class="see-also"><p>See also: <a href="http://mashable.com/2013/04/16/iphone-concept-videos/" data-crackerjax="#post-slider">7 iPhone Concepts That'll Make You Salivate</a></p></div>
<p>In his video, embedded below, Keller notes that only the flash appears changed on the new 5S (it's now rectangular). The headphone jack and Lightning port remain on the bottom, and the volume rockers and mute switch have the same design as they do on the 5.</p>
