Joking Bad: Watch Jimmy Fallon's 'Breaking Bad' Parody

   18.5k
   Shares
   [44]Share
   [45]Tweet
   [46]Share
   What's This?
   [47]Stan-schroeder
   By Stan Schroeder2013-09-12 07:22:24 UTC

   IFRAME: [48]https://www.youtube.com/embed/duKL2dAJN6I

   Breaking Bad fans, take notice: Jimmy Fallon has unveiled his take on
   the series, called "Joking Bad."

   Sporting the signature green shirt, white undies and bald head, Fallon
   is making fat bucks dealing jokes. He does this with the help of
   announcer Steve Higgins who plays Jesse (yes, he mentions the b-word a
   couple dozen times); Late Night head writer A.D. Miles as Hank, and Bob
   Odenkirk as his Breaking Bad character, Saul.

   When Fallon runs out of jokes for his show, though, Bryan Cranston and
   Aaron Paul are there to boo him off the stage.

   See also: [49]Tread Lightly Through These 17 'Breaking Bad' Inspired
   Vines

   There are a couple of more surprises in the 13-minute clip, and just
   enough easter eggs from the series to satisfy the fans.

   Fallon's "Joking Bad" comes shortly after AMC announced a Breaking Bad
   spinoff, called [50]"Better Call Saul." The show will be a prequel to
   Breaking Bad and will focus on the popular character from the show,
   lawyer Saul Goodman.

   How do you like Fallon's take on Walter White? Share your thoughts in
   the comments.
