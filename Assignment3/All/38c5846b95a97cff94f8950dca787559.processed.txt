Twitter Files for IPO

   3.8k
   Shares
   [44]Share
   [45]Tweet
   [46]Share
   What's This?
   Costolo
   [47]Todd-wasserman-headshot-sml
   By Todd Wasserman2013-09-12 21:07:53 UTC

   [48]Twitter has filed initial paperwork to go public, the company
   revealed on Thursday. The company tweeted its announcement on Thursday
   afternoon:

   See also: [49]Twitter's IPO Filing: What It Means

     We've confidentially submitted an S-1 to the SEC for a planned IPO.
     This Tweet does not constitute an offer of any securities for sale.

     -- Twitter (@twitter) [50]September 12, 2013

   Shortly after, Twitter added:

     Now, back to work. [51]pic.twitter.com/e4lK8e7pY9

     -- Twitter (@twitter) [52]September 12, 2013

   Twitter is currently valued at around [53]$11 billion, according to
   estimates. Twitter is able to file confidentially thanks to the JOBS
   [Jumpstart Our Business Startups] Act, which lets companies go public
   without public scrutiny. JOBS applies to companies with less than $1
   billion in revenues.

10 Tweets That Rocked the Twitter-verse

    1. Daed0082

The Hudson Plane Landing
       When a commercial flight was forced to make an [54]emergency
       landing in the Hudson River, Twitter -- with the help of image
       service Twitpic -- told the story.
    2. 1f96528f

Kenneth Cole's #Cairo Blunder
       During a rash of riots in the Middle East and North Africa, Twitter
       played a role in protesters' fight for democracy. But a crass tweet
       from [55]Kenneth Cole stands out as a hallmark of insensitivity.
    3. Aa5382e7

Bieber Tweets "His" Digits
       In what was called "kind of a jerk move," teen idol Justin Bieber
       [56]tweeted the phone number of an invasive fan as if it were his
       own. Said fan's phone was ringing off the hook for hours and
       received at least 26,000 text messages.
    4. 968a5a13

Marriage Proposal
       [57]Social media proposals are a whole thing unto themselves these
       days, but the trend probably started with this sweet tweet.
    5. 4771c86b

Ice: The Final Frontier
       Tweets from space are all the rage. The landmark discovery of ice
       on Mars caused quite the stir on Twitter.
    6. A938a7a2

"Arrested"
       This single-word tweet ended up being a lifesaver for [58]UC
       Berkeley journalism student James Buck.
    7. F564e47d

Jack's First Tweet ...
       Twitter co-founder Jack Dorsey's first tweet -- the inauspicious
       blurb that started it all back in 2006.
    8. 1db0a49a

... and Oprah's First Tweet
       When the talk show host [59]discussed Twitter with Biz Stone and Ev
       Williams, she brought mainstream attention to a rather geek-centric
       web app. [60]Twitter adoption spiked as a consequence.
    9. A6fa2e1b

Tweets From Space: Now In Real-Time!
       The [61]first live and real-time tweet from space came from the
       International Space Station in early 2010.
   10. 02f4f787

The Abbottabad Tweeter
       Without knowing exactly what was going on, Twitter user
       @ReallyVirtual started tweeting about [62]a helicopter crash in his
       town. The town happened to be Abbottabad, and the crash happened to
       be one event in the raid that led to Osama bin Laden's death.
