<section class='article-content'>

<p><a href="http://mashable.com/category/Yahoo">Yahoo</a> has launched a complete redesign of its dated <a href="http://mashable.com/follow/topics/My-Yahoo">My Yahoo</a>.</p>
<p>It's been more than six years since the company touched My Yahoo, the homepage customization concept, but it's timing for a new look couldn't be more perfect: Google announced last year it's <a href="http://mashable.com/2013/05/20/yahoo-flickr-event/">pulling the plug on similar service iGoogle</a>, so loyal users may be looking for a new place to go come November. </p>
<div class="see-also"><p>See also: <a href="http://mashable.com/2013/09/17/what-to-do-before-ios-7/" data-crackerjax="#post-slider">What to Do Before Downloading iOS 7</a></p></div>
<p>This is the latest of many redesign efforts we've seen from Yahoo this year, joining the overhaul of its homepage, <a href="http://mashable.com/2013/05/20/yahoo-flickr-event/" data-crackerjax="#post-slider">its photo-sharing site Flickr</a> and most recently, <a href="http://mashable.com/2013/09/05/yahoo-new-logo-2/" data-crackerjax="#post-slider">the logo</a>. Under the direction of CEO Marissa Mayer, the move is part of an effort to make the once-struggling company more relevant and modern. </p>
<p><img src="http://rack.2.mshcdn.com/media/ZgkyMDEzLzA5LzE4LzVjL1lhaG9vRGVzaWduLjY5OGJjLmpwZwpwCXRodW1iCTEyMDB4OTYwMD4/ac26ea10/527/YahooDesign-640x363.jpg" alt="YahooDesign" class="" data-width="640" data-fragment="m!fe6e" data-image="http://rack.2.mshcdn.com/media/ZgkyMDEzLzA5LzE4LzVjL1lhaG9vRGVzaWduLjY5OGJjLmpwZwpwCXRodW1iCTEyMDB4OTYwMD4/ac26ea10/527/YahooDesign-640x363.jpg" data-micro="1"></p>
<p>For those unfamiliar with the feature, My Yahoo acts as a portal to things you're interested in accessing online, from email accounts and calendars to stocks, sports scores, weather and content from around the web such as recipes and news articles. </p>
<p>"We have a loyal, existing user base of people who use My Yahoo every day, and we think there is a way to really grow the platform," Mike Kerns, senior VP of homepage and vertical products, told <em>Mashable</em>. "When we launched a new homepage design in February, we learned that every feature might not be for everyone and wanted to give more options to personalize their experience."</p>
<p>At first glance, you'll notice the new My Yahoo is much more streamlined and organized. You can customize the content you want, by dragging and dropping columns and modules across the page. It's also very fast and responsive. </p>
<p><img src="http://rack.2.mshcdn.com/media/ZgkyMDEzLzA5LzE4L2MzL015WWFob282NDB4LmEwNmNhLmpwZwpwCXRodW1iCTEyMDB4OTYwMD4/253cd8d0/d03/MyYahoo-640x363.jpg" alt="MyYahoo" class="" data-width="640" data-fragment="m!3c62" data-image="http://rack.2.mshcdn.com/media/ZgkyMDEzLzA5LzE4L2MzL015WWFob282NDB4LmEwNmNhLmpwZwpwCXRodW1iCTEyMDB4OTYwMD4/253cd8d0/d03/MyYahoo-640x363.jpg" data-micro="1"></p>
<p>Another new touch is the introduction of themes from designers such as Rachel Zoe to bands such as Fun. and One Direction. This allows users to give a personalized feel to their homepage, rather than Yahoo's standard page.</p>
<p><img src="http://rack.2.mshcdn.com/media/ZgkyMDEzLzA5LzE4LzFhL1lhaG9vQ2F0ZWdvLjk4YTA5LmpwZwpwCXRodW1iCTEyMDB4OTYwMD4/3c8d1d2f/8aa/YahooCategories-640x363.jpg" alt="My Yahoo" class="" data-width="640" data-fragment="m!f813" data-image="http://rack.2.mshcdn.com/media/ZgkyMDEzLzA5LzE4LzFhL1lhaG9vQ2F0ZWdvLjk4YTA5LmpwZwpwCXRodW1iCTEyMDB4OTYwMD4/3c8d1d2f/8aa/YahooCategories-640x363.jpg" data-micro="1"></p>
<p>Kerns said the company placed a big emphasis on making My Yahoo optimized for mobile too, so users can get customized content on the go.   </p>
<p>"My Yahoo hasn't kept up with the times, and we wanted to make it much more accessible on mobile, so you can get everything you want by accessing one main page and make that a part of a daily habit," he added.</p>
<p>Yahoo makes it simple for iGoogle users to import their preferences; after making the transfer, the company will build a personalized My Yahoo page for you.</p>
<p>"Google announced it is shutting down iGoogle in November and we thought this could be an opportunity to bring over users who are still looking for a rich customized experience," Kerns said. "We're trying to make the switch easy for everyone." </p>
