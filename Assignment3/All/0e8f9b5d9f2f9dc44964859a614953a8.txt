Not only do they help you connect with your readers, they can also inspire later blog entries. The challenge, oftentimes, is following all the conversations around your content</span><span style="background-color: white; color: #444444; font-family: Arial; font-size: 15px; vertical-align: baseline; white-space: pre-wrap;">&#8212;</span><span style="font-family: Arial; font-size: 15px; vertical-align: baseline; white-space: pre-wrap;">on Google+, for instance, as well as on your website. <div dir="ltr" style="line-height: 1.15; margin-bottom: 0pt; margin-top: 0pt;">
<b id="docs-internal-guid-7e0c0d97-1b9c-540b-d584-4baa1397696e" style="font-weight: normal; line-height: normal;"><span style="font-family: Arial; font-size: 15px; vertical-align: baseline; white-space: pre-wrap;">Starting today, you can bring Google+ Comments to your Blogger blog. <b id="docs-internal-guid-7e0c0d97-1b9c-540b-d584-4baa1397696e" style="font-weight: normal; line-height: normal;">
