<p><em><strong>Warning:</strong> This post may negatively impact your <em>Grand Theft Auto V </em>experience with spoilers.</em></p>
<p>When Rockstar Games announced <em><a href="http://mashable.com/category/grand-theft-auto-v/">Grand Theft Auto V</a></em>, the company promised the most expansive game world ever. The game's map — <a href="http://www.reddit.com/r/GrandTheftAutoV/comments/1m3h8b/mustresistdontwanttospoil/" target="_blank">leaked</a> on Reddit — confirms Rockstar kept its promise. Los Santos appears really big in the map included in <em>GTA V</em> packaging.</p>
<p><a href="http://mashable.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/09/Los-Santos.jpg"><img src="http://rack.1.mshcdn.com/media/ZgkyMDEzLzA5LzExLzc5L0xvc1NhbnRvcy44MDZlMi5qcGcKcAl0aHVtYgkxMjAweDk2MDA-/7f6aa1d9/d78/Los-Santos.jpg" alt="Los Santos" class="" data-width="640" data-fragment="m!56a7" data-image="http://rack.1.mshcdn.com/media/ZgkyMDEzLzA5LzExLzc5L0xvc1NhbnRvcy44MDZlMi5qcGcKcAl0aHVtYgkxMjAweDk2MDA-/7f6aa1d9/d78/Los-Santos.jpg" data-micro="1"></a></p>
<p>The southern portion of the map shows a sprawling city with an expansive Los Santos County surrounding it. We knew from previously released information that lots of activities can happen in the outer reaches of Los Santos County, such as racing, skydiving and more.</p>
