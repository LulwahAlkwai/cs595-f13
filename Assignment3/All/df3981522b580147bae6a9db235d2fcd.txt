<p>The first barrage of winged-woman <a href="http://mashable.com/category/emmys/">Emmy</a> statuettes were handed out at the Primetime Creative Arts Emmy on Sept. 15 (airing on FXX on Sept. 21), and <a href="http://mashable.com/category/netflix/">Netflix</a> won the night's first one for <em>House of Cards</em> in the Outstanding Casting for a Drama Series category. </p>
<p>The video-streaming service's hit political drama — up for nine Primetime Emmys this season — beat out <em>Downton Abbey</em>, <em>Game of Thrones</em>, <em>Homeland </em>and <em>The Good Wife</em>. </p>
<div class="see-also"><p>See also: <a href="http://mashable.com/2013/08/23/kevin-spacey-tv-speech/" data-crackerjax="#post-slider">Kevin Spacey Warns TV Networks: Change or Get Left Behind</a></p></div>
<p>This is Netflix's first Primetime Creative Arts Emmy; Netflix previously snagged two engineering Emmys, Netflix confirmed to <em>Mashable</em>. Held a week before the <a href="http://mashable.com/2013/07/18/emmys-nominations-nominees/">65th Primetime Emmy Awards</a>, the Creative Arts ceremony honors behind-the-scenes achievements. </p>
<p>Casting directors Laray Mayfield and Julie Schubert are credited for the win. The cast includes Kevin Spacey, Robin Wright, Kate Mara, Corey Stoll, Michael Kelly, Sakina Jaffrey, Kristen Connolly and Constance Zimmer.</p>
<p><strong>Update:</strong> Netflix also won for Outstanding Cinematography for a Single Camera Series for a Drama, defeating <em>Breaking Bad</em>, <em>Game of Thrones</em> and <em>Mad Men</em>.</p>
<p>"We are so proud that both Casting and Cinematography were recognized with these historic Emmy wins for <em>House of Cards</em> and Netflix," Ted Sarandos, Netflix's chief content officer, said in a statement.  "Laray Mayfield, Julie Schubert abd Eigil Bryld's work was truly exquisite, and the Academy's  acknowledgement for their work is richly deserved."</p>
<p>For next week's Primetime Emmys, the Academy of Television Arts &amp; Sciences has pit Netflix against the traditional TV players in many categories (see list below). <a href="http://mashable.com/category/netflix/">Netflix</a> made history this year when the television academy nominated for the first time ever an original series hosted online instead of on cable or network TV. </p>
<p>The Creative Arts Emmys is being taped today but will air Sept. 21 at 9 p.m. ET. The Primetime Emmys air live Sept. 22 at 8 p.m. ET on <a href="http://mashable.com/category/cbs/">CBS</a> from the Nokia Theatre in L.A. </p>
<p><iframe width="960" height="720" src="//www.youtube.com/embed/hBz7e6E_ttI" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen></iframe></p>
<p>Three Netflix shows earned a total of <a href="http://mashable.com/2013/07/18/emmys-nominations-nominees/" data-crackerjax="#post-slider">14 nods</a>: political drama <em>House of Cards</em> (9), the new season of <em>Arrested Development</em> (3) and horror thriller <em>Hemlock Grove</em> (2). </p>
<p>Here's a rundown of Netflix's nominations:</p>

<li><p>Outstanding Casting for a Drama Series</p></li>
<li><p>Outstanding Directing for a Drama Series</p></li>
<li><p>Outstanding Original Main Title Theme Music</p></li>
<li><p>Outstanding Music Composition for a Series (Original Dramatic Score)</p></li>
<li><p>Outstanding Cinematography for a Single-Camera Series</p></li>
