id="dd_start"></a><p><img
class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-25179" alt="Neil head on Bill shoulder 23" src="http://hbowatch.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/09/Neil-head-on-Bill-shoulder-23.jpg" width="600" height="400" title="Neil head on Bill shoulder 23" /></p><p>REAL TIME WITH BILL MAHER returns from hiatus and resumes its 11th season FRIDAY, SEPT. 13 (10:00-11:00 p.m. live ET/tape-delayed PT), with a replay at 11:00 p.m., exclusively on HBO. Allowing Maher to offer his unique perspective on contemporary issues, the show includes an opening monologue, roundtable discussions with panelists, and interviews with in-studio and satellite guests. Emmy® winning scientist Bill Nye is the mid-show interview guest. The roundtable guests are economist Zanny Minton-Beddoes, former Lt. Governor Michael Steele and editor Matt Taibbi. The top-of-show interview guest will be announced when confirmed.</p><p><strong>HBOWatch</strong>: Well, I&#8217;m betting <a
href="http://www.reddit.com/r/atheism/">/r/atheism</a> and <a
href="http://www.reddit.com/r/science/">/r/science</a> are equally happy today. Bill Nye is a god amongst men in certain circles and we can&#8217;t wait to hear what he has to say on the issues of the day. <a
href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Neil_deGrasse_Tyson">Neil Degrasse Tyson</a> is also a fan favorite scientist and friend of Real Time. His <a
href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Neil_deGrasse_Tyson">previous appearances</a> were very entertaining as he spoke truth to stupid. Let&#8217;s hope Mr. Nye can do the same.</p><p>We&#8217;ve all been eager to hear Bill&#8217;s thoughts on Syria and the other issues that have arisen since his break started this summer. Tune in this Friday for all the goods.</p><p>We&#8217;ll update this post when we know who the top of the show guest is.</p> <a
