                            <h2><a href="http://blog.ifttt.com/post/55130449805/the-power-of-ifttt-now-in-your-pocket">The power of IFTTT, now in your pocket</a></h2>
                            <div class="date">Wednesday July 10th, 2013</div>
                            <a class="tag-link" href="http://blog.ifttt.com/tagged/Milestones">Milestones</a><a class="tag-link" href="http://blog.ifttt.com/tagged/Updates">Updates</a>
                            <div class="post-content">
                                <p><span>Soon the phone will be just as valuable in your pocket as it is in your hand. Idle phones move data, respond to new locations, and receive notifications — this is only the beginning.</span></p>
<p>Today, we’re thrilled to introduce IFTTT for iPhone.</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://ifttt.com/mobile" title="IFTTT | Mobile" target="_blank"><img alt="image" height="331px;" src="https://lh4.googleusercontent.com/nW6BYQygTX3XDCIASycsbsdokxROC-i5LeYyxECdvDvkESsOMwNX8I7k3tKvw7J2kqkn3CPbjAI8cvcXHMKNYbFMEPdg1JizZXeAUQyDb_-VDiwSxXVDL3vW" width="331px;"/></a><span></span></p>
<p><a href="https://itunes.apple.com/us/app/ifttt/id660944635" title="Download IFTTT for iPhone from the App Store" target="_blank">Download the app</a> and unlock three new Channels for iPhone: <a href="https://ifttt.com/ios_contacts" title="iOS Contacts Channel" target="_blank">Contacts</a>, <a href="https://ifttt.com/ios_photos" title="iOS Photos Channel" target="_blank">Photos</a>, and <a href="https://ifttt.com/ios_reminders" title="iOS Reminders Channel" target="_blank">Reminders</a> (with more to come). Create and use Recipes around the things you do on the go: snapping screenshots, adding new contacts, completing reminders, and organizing your photo albums.</p>
<p>IFTTT for iPhone is our first step towards bringing the power of IFTTT to all of your devices. <a href="https://ifttt.com/mobile" title="IFTTT | Mobile" target="_blank">Put the iPhone to work for you</a>.</p>
<p><span></span><span>—The IFTTT Team</span></p>
