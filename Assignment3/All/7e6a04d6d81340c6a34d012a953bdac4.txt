<div class="shift-to-hero"><p><iframe allowfullscreen="" frameborder="0" height="360" src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/duKL2dAJN6I" width="640"></iframe></p></div>
<p><em>Breaking Bad</em> fans, take notice: Jimmy Fallon has unveiled his take on the series, called "Joking Bad." </p>
<p>Sporting the signature green shirt, white undies and bald head, Fallon is making fat bucks dealing jokes. He does this with the help of announcer Steve Higgins who plays Jesse (yes, he mentions the b-word a couple dozen times); Late Night head writer A.D. Miles as Hank, and Bob Odenkirk as his <em>Breaking Bad</em> character, Saul.  </p>
<p>When Fallon runs out of jokes for his show, though, Bryan Cranston and Aaron Paul are there to boo him off the stage. </p>
<div class="see-also"><p>See also: <a href="http://mashable.com/2013/08/25/breaking-bad-vine/" data-crackerjax="#post-slider">Tread Lightly Through These 17 'Breaking Bad' Inspired Vines</a></p></div>
<p>There are a couple of more surprises in the 13-minute clip, and just enough easter eggs from the series to satisfy the fans. </p>
