New Harry Potter film! New Harry Potter film?  SEP 12 2013

   Well, sort of. [2]Rowling is writing a screenplay for Warner Bros.
   based on a Hogwart's textbook called Fantastic Beasts and Where to Find
   Them.

     Although it will be set in the worldwide community of witches and
     wizards where I was so happy for seventeen years, 'Fantastic Beasts
     and Where to Find Them' is neither a prequel nor a sequel to the
     Harry Potter series, but an extension of the wizarding world. The
     laws and customs of the hidden magical society will be familiar to
     anyone who has read the Harry Potter books or seen the films, but
     Newt's story will start in New York, seventy years before Harry's
     gets underway.
