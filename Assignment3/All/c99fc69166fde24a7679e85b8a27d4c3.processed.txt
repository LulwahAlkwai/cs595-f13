Theory Jobs 2013

   Time for the annual spring jobs posts. Like [5]last year, I set up
   a [6]Google Spreadsheet that everyone can edit so we can crowd source
   who is going where next year.
   A reminder of the rules
     * I set up separate sheets for faculty, industry, postdoc/visitors
       and students.
     * People should be connected to theoretical computer science, broadly
       defined.
     * Only add jobs that you are absolutely sure have been offered and
       accepted. This is not the place for speculation and rumors.
     * You are welcome to add yourself, or people your department has
       hired.

   This document will continue to grow as more jobs settle. So check it
   often.
