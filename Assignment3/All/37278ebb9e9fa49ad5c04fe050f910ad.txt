									<h2><span>Announcing the Devcup 2013 Category Winners</span></h2>

	<div class="short-social">

		<div class="fb-like" data-send="false" data-href="http://blog.evernote.com/blog/2013/08/02/announcing-the-devcup-2013-category-winners/" data-layout="button_count" data-width="450" data-show-faces="false" data-font="arial"></div>

		<a href="https://twitter.com/share" class="twitter-share-button" data-url="http://blog.evernote.com/blog/2013/08/02/announcing-the-devcup-2013-category-winners/"data-text="Announcing the Devcup 2013 Category Winners" data-hashtags="evernote,blog">Tweet</a>

	<a class="comments" href="http://blog.evernote.com/blog/2013/08/02/announcing-the-devcup-2013-category-winners/#disqus_thread" data-disqus-identifier="http://blog.evernote.com/blog/2013/08/02/announcing-the-devcup-2013-category-winners/">Comment

					<div class="thumbnail">
													<img width="640" height="227" src="http://blog.evernote.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/featured-devcup-banner-2.png" class="attachment-featuredimage wp-post-image" alt="featured-devcup-banner-2" />											</div>
					<div class="art-content"><p>There are a million things you can do with Evernote, but Evernote is just one app. It can&#8217;t do it all. Fortunately, an active community of developers and designers is constantly coming up with new ideas, apps, and services that extend Evernote&#8217;s capabilities for users.</p>
<p>The creativity of these developers peaks every year when we run our annual developer competition, <a href="http://devcup.evernote.com/" target="_blank">the Devcup</a>. This year, teams submitted over 180 apps and services to answer the challenge. Some are beautiful, some are incredibly creative, some are technically amazing. A few are all of the above.</p>
<p>To reward the best Devcup entries, we are awarding prizes in categories for popular Evernote use cases, plus a few for special technical or design achievements. These apps getting awards provide tremendous benefit to Evernote&#8217;s users, and they showcase what the Evernote platform can really do.</p>
<p>Each of the teams behind these winners will receive passes to attend EC3, <a href="http://evernote.com/ec" target="_blank">the third annual Evernote Conference</a>. At EC3, teams will have the opportunity to connect with Evernote engineers and designers, and to present their projects to attending investors and media. In addition, each category team will receive a $1000 credit for Amazon Web Services to continue hosting their growing apps.</p>
<p><strong><em>We are proud to present the following projects as the winners of the<br />
Devcup 2013 Category Prizes:</em></strong></p>
<h3>Best Evernote Business Integration: <a href="https://www.hackerleague.org/hackathons/evernote-devcup-2013/hacks/smartgtd" target="_blank">SmartTM</a></h3>
<p><a href="http://www.smartgtd.com/"><i><img class="size-full wp-image-35146 alignleft" alt="SmartTM" src="http://blog.evernote.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/smarttm.png" width="125" height="125" /></i></a><a href="https://www.hackerleague.org/hackathons/evernote-devcup-2013/hacks/smartgtd" target="_blank"><b>SmartTM</b></a>, Poland &#8211; <a href="http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=hNR_SM37wBY" target="_blank"><i>Watch the video</i></a><br />
SmartTM is a task organizer built around the &#8220;Getting Things Done&#8221; methodology. SmartTM sports a tight integration with Evernote, including hooking into the Reminders API and into Business notebooks. SmartTM is a powerful, multi-featured tool designed specifically for business users.<br />
<b></b></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<h3>Design Achievement Award: <a href="https://www.hackerleague.org/hackathons/evernote-devcup-2013/hacks/bubble-browser-for-ipad" target="_blank">Bubble Browser</a></h3>
<p><a href="http://bubblebrowserapp.com/"><b><i><img class="size-full wp-image-35137 alignleft" alt="Bubble Browser" src="http://blog.evernote.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/bb.png" width="125" height="125" /></i></b></a><a href="https://www.hackerleague.org/hackathons/evernote-devcup-2013/hacks/bubble-browser-for-ipad" target="_blank"><b>Bubble Browser</b></a>, Poland &#8211; <a href="http://vimeo.com/69616334" target="_blank"><i>Watch the video</i></a><br />
Bubble Browser is a visualization app for your Evernote notebook. It is a beautiful visual search tool for your Evernote account: Its sleek and intuitive user experience will encourage you to play with your Evernote data and discover content and connections that you might not find otherwise.<br />
<b></b></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<h3>Best Technical Integration: <a href="https://www.hackerleague.org/hackathons/evernote-devcup-2013/hacks/hop-dot-in" target="_blank">Hop.in</a></h3>
<p><a href="https://hop.in/"><img class="size-full wp-image-35142 alignleft" alt="hopin" src="http://blog.evernote.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/hopin.png" width="125" height="125" /></a><b><a href="https://www.hackerleague.org/hackathons/evernote-devcup-2013/hacks/hop-dot-in" target="_blank">Hop.in</a></b>, Chile &#8211; <a href="http://vimeo.com/69594106" target="_blank"><i>Watch the video</i></a><br />
Hop.in is a mobile browser and web clipper designed to grab specific content on the web to sync with an Evernote account. Hop.in allows users to specify the amount of content they clip, and is built to isolate multimedia items for clipping. Hop.in&#8217;s smart clipping technology is a clear technical achievement for this year&#8217;s Devcup.<br />
<b></b></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<h3>Best for Education &amp; Research: <a href="https://www.hackerleague.org/hackathons/evernote-devcup-2013/hacks/biscuit" target="_blank">biscuit</a></h3>
<p><a href="http://getbiscuit.com/en/"><img class="size-full wp-image-35138 alignleft" alt="biscuit" src="http://blog.evernote.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/biscuit.png" width="125" height="125" /></a><b><a href="https://www.hackerleague.org/hackathons/evernote-devcup-2013/hacks/biscuit" target="_blank">biscuit</a></b>, South Korea &#8211; <a href="http://vimeo.com/69725095" target="_blank"><i>Watch the video</i></a><br />
Biscuit is a simple translation app that allows users to clip words from their mobile devices. It quickly translates words into multiple languages, and can sync to Evernote in a study-friendly format. Biscuit is the perfect education tool for Evernote users looking for a quick language reference.<br />
<b></b></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<h3>Best for Family &amp; Parenting: <a href="https://www.hackerleague.org/hackathons/evernote-devcup-2013/hacks/babywaa" target="_blank">BabyWaa</a></h3>
<p><a href="http://babywaa.com/"><img class="size-full wp-image-35150 alignleft" alt="Babywaa" src="http://blog.evernote.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/babywaa1.png" width="125" height="125" /></a><b><a href="https://www.hackerleague.org/hackathons/evernote-devcup-2013/hacks/babywaa" target="_blank">BabyWaa</a></b>, South Korea &#8211; <a href="http://vimeo.com/69606142" target="_blank"><i>Watch the video</i></a><br />
BabyWaa is a multiplatform app that allows parents to record moments of their newborns&#8217; lives. Its social network functions let parents share photos and content, and then sync their favorite moments to Evernote for permanent safekeeping.<br />
<b></b></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<h3>Best for Food &amp; Cooking: <a href="https://www.hackerleague.org/hackathons/evernote-devcup-2013/hacks/grocerytrip" target="_blank">Grocerytrip</a></h3>
<p><a href="http://www.hcrisp.com/grocerytrip"><img class="size-full wp-image-35148 alignleft" alt="Grocery Trip" src="http://blog.evernote.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/grocerytrip.png" width="125" height="125" /></a><b><a href="https://www.hackerleague.org/hackathons/evernote-devcup-2013/hacks/grocerytrip" target="_blank">Grocerytrip</a></b>, United States &#8211; <a href="http://vimeo.com/69006480" target="_blank"><i>Watch the video</i></a><br />
Grocerytrip is a smart, mobile organizer for your grocery lists. Clip recipes or items from the web and Grocerytrip automatically figures out the items you need to buy. Grocerytrip creates shopping lists that sync with Evernote, simplifying your next trip to the market.<br />
<b></b></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<h3>Paperless Achievement Award: <a href="https://www.hackerleague.org/hackathons/evernote-devcup-2013/hacks/lightly" target="_blank">Lightly</a></h3>
<p><a href="http://lightlyapp.com/"><img class="size-full wp-image-35152 alignleft" alt="lightlyapp" src="http://blog.evernote.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/lightlyapp.png" width="125" height="125" /></a><b><a href="https://www.hackerleague.org/hackathons/evernote-devcup-2013/hacks/lightly" target="_blank">Lightly</a></b>, Hong Kong &#8211; <a href="http://vimeo.com/69529006" target="_blank"><i>Watch the video</i></a><br />
Lightly is a mobile web clipper and highlighter app that allows users to sync online content to their Evernote accounts. Lightly&#8217;s smooth clipping and highlighting features give users both the content and the context, replacing the need to use paper articles as physical memory devices.<br />
<b></b></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<h3>Best for Photography: <a href="https://www.hackerleague.org/hackathons/evernote-devcup-2013/hacks/cooliris" target="_blank">Cooliris</a></h3>
<p><a href="http://www.cooliris.com/"><img class="size-full wp-image-35140 alignleft" alt="cooliris" src="http://blog.evernote.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/cooliris.png" width="125" height="125" /></a><a href="https://www.hackerleague.org/hackathons/evernote-devcup-2013/hacks/cooliris" target="_blank"><b>Cooliris</b></a>, United States &#8211; <a href="http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=lAO2Ltwf_rE" target="_blank"><i>Watch the video</i></a><br />
Cooliris is a photo browser for your mobile devices and the Web. It syncs with Evernote and other cloud platforms, and allows users to easily share photos from the app&#8217;s chat feature. Cooliris is a clean and useful photo integration for Evernote users.<br />
<b></b></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<h3>Best for Productivity: <a href="https://www.hackerleague.org/hackathons/evernote-devcup-2013/hacks/context-booster" target="_blank">Context Booster</a></h3>
<p><a href="http://www.contextbooster.com/"><img class="size-full wp-image-35139 alignleft" alt="context_booster" src="http://blog.evernote.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/context_booster.png" width="125" height="125" /></a><b><a href="https://www.hackerleague.org/hackathons/evernote-devcup-2013/hacks/context-booster" target="_blank">Context Booster</a></b>, Germany &#8211; <a href="http://vimeo.com/69763613" target="_blank"><i>Watch the video</i></a><br />
Context Booster is an intelligent knowledge assistant for Evernote. This app scours the web for content related to your Evernote notes. It can populate your notebooks with additional information that it links to the notes that you write, making it a great productivity tool for research and writing.<br />
<b></b></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<h3>Best for Publishing: <a href="https://www.hackerleague.org/hackathons/evernote-devcup-2013/hacks/postach-dot-io" target="_blank">Postach.io</a></h3>
<p><a href="http://postach.io/"><img class="size-full wp-image-35145 alignleft" alt="postachio" src="http://blog.evernote.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/postachio.png" width="125" height="125" /></a><a href="https://www.hackerleague.org/hackathons/evernote-devcup-2013/hacks/postach-dot-io" target="_blank"><b>Postach.io</b></a>, Canada &#8211; <a href="http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=xCthUlkc7V8" target="_blank"><i>Watch the video</i></a><br />
Postach.io is a powerful blogging tool that posts content directly from your Evernote account. Simply tag a finished blog post with &#8220;published&#8221; and your content will appear on your blog. Postach.io sports an easy, personal publishing experience, and has several attractive blog layouts.<br />
<b></b></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<h3>Best Travel App: <a href="https://www.hackerleague.org/hackathons/evernote-devcup-2013/hacks/xing" target="_blank">Xing</a></h3>
<p><a href="http://getxing.com/statics/index.html"><img class="size-full wp-image-35147 alignleft" alt="xing" src="http://blog.evernote.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/XingAppIcon175.png" width="125" height="125" /></a><a href="https://www.hackerleague.org/hackathons/evernote-devcup-2013/hacks/xing" target="_blank"><b>Xing</b></a>, Taiwan &#8211; <a href="http://vimeo.com/69587225" target="_blank"><i>Watch the video</i></a><br />
Xing is a location-based travel app with a stunning design and interface. It integrates with your Evernote account, syncing with notebooks for your various trips. Xing also reminds you of travel items when you have reached a location. It&#8217;s a great traveling companion.<br />
<b></b></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<h3>Best for Collaboration: <a href="https://www.hackerleague.org/hackathons/evernote-devcup-2013/hacks/mural-dot-ly" target="_blank">Mural.ly</a></h3>
<p><a href="https://mural.ly/"><img class="size-full wp-image-35144 alignleft" alt="murally-icon" src="http://blog.evernote.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/murally-icon.png" width="125" height="125" /></a><b><a href="https://www.hackerleague.org/hackathons/evernote-devcup-2013/hacks/mural-dot-ly" target="_blank">Mural.ly</a></b>, Argentina &#8211; <a href="http://vimeo.com/69773814" target="_blank"><i>Watch the video</i></a><br />
<a href="http://Mural.ly" target="_blank">Mural.ly</a> is multi-featured collaboration tool that allows for virtual whiteboarding and the online development of ideas. Users can grab content from their Evernote account and work with ease in teams. <a href="http://Mural.ly" target="_blank">Mural.ly</a> is a creative business tool, making collaborative brainstorming easy.<br />
<b></b></p>

<b> </b><br />
There are more Devcup prizes right around the corner. <a target="_blank">On Friday, August 16th</a>, our three major partners for the year of developer activities &#8212; Docomo Innovation Ventures, Honda Silicon Valley Lab, and Wayra Accelerator &#8212; will each announce which teams they are sponsoring to receive EC3 passes in recognition for their excellent work.</p>
<p>Also on August 16th we will announce which category winners will be the six Devcup finalists. These teams will be flown out to join us at EC3, and will be able to pitch their apps to all the conference attendees.</p>
<p>Congratulations again to all of this year&#8217;s category winners. We look forward to seeing all the teams at the Evernote Conference in September. Join us at EC3 this year, where we will announce the final gold, silver and bronze winners of the Devcup, as well as the names of all the teams that will be joining us at the Evernote Accelerator in October.</p>
