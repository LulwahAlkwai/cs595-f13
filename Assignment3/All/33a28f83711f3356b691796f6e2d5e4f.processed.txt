Turn Your Smartphone Into a Microscope With Single Lens

   3.4k
   Shares
   [44]Share
   [45]Tweet
   [46]Share
   What's This?
   [47]297153_3873833284611_1494937766_n
   By Sara Afzal2013-09-13 20:12:26 UTC

   Smartphone users who are itching to photograph objects too small for
   the naked eye can rest assured -- the Micro Phone Lens turns your
   [48]iPhone or Android camera phone into a portable handheld microscope.

   The Micro Phone Lens is a soft lens that sticks directly onto the
   camera lens on the back of your phone. Users can then zoom into 15X
   magnification in high focus. The minimum camera requirement is 5
   megapixels.

   See also: [49]Use These 10 Apps During Downtime

   At the time of writing, the [50]Kickstarter campaign for the lens
   exceeded its $5,000 goal nearly 20 times over. Creator Thomas Larson
   told Mashable that the [51]project reached its goal in just over one
   day.

   iPhone next to penny

   Backers who contribute $15 or more will receive a Micro PHone Lens and
   plastic carrying case.

   After fulfilling the Kickstarter orders, Larson would eventually like
   to make the lenses available on [52]Amazon. The University of
   Washington graduate has been working on the business plan for the Micro
   Phone Lens for over a year.

   In the future, Larson also plans to release a 150X lens, which will
   have even more precise microscope capabilities.

   "In many ways, this Kickstarter campaign was just a warm up for the
   150X. I think it has much more potential, potential which enough to
   change the world," he said. "Having a cheap available microscope that
   can transmit images would be of immense use for healthcare in the
   developing world."
