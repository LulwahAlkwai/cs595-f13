<p><span style="color:#2b6348;"><strong>COMMUNICATE VALUE, MANAGE CHANGE</strong></span></p>
<p>Lundberg Media is a communication services and advisory company specializing in the intersection of business and technology. Services include:</p>
<p><a title="Services" href="http://lundbergmedia.com/services/">Consulting</a>              <a title="Editorial" href="http://lundbergmedia.com/editorial/">Editorial</a>             <a title="Speaking" href="http://lundbergmedia.com/speaking/">Workshops &amp; Events</a></p>
<p>The pace of technology change is dizzying. But most organizations are designed for stability, not change. CIOs and technology marketers share a common challenge: how to communicate the real benefits and risks of new technologies and speed adoption in a responsible way.</p>
<p>Lundberg Media helps leaders and their teams translate complex issues into focused, compelling messages to gain buy in and deliver results. Whether introducing a new technology, delivering a new capability or entering a new market, meaningful communication is more important than ever to companies’ ability to influence stakeholder thinking and behavior — both inside and outside the company. Led by longtime former <em>CIO</em> magazine Editor in Chief Abbie Lundberg, Lundberg Media is uniquely positioned to help leaders and their teams develop powerful and effective communication programs.</p>
