	<article class="article">
		The world is anxiously awaiting the arrival of "Call of Duty: Ghosts" on Nov. 5. Our friends at Activision today outlined a range of great options for Xbox fans &ndash; everything from Xbox One upgrades for just $10, to information on keeping your Season Pass content, profile, and stats with you as you transition to "Call of Duty: Ghosts."<br />
<br />
<span style="font-size: 15px; line-height: 1.5;">More info from Activision below.&nbsp;</span><span style="font-size: 15px; line-height: 22px;"><br />
<br />
</span><span style="line-height: 1.5; font-size: 18px;"><strong>Xbox One Upgrade Offers</strong><br />
</span><br />
<span style="font-size: 15px; line-height: 1.5;">Gamers can purchase a disc-less dual-license digital version of "Call of Duty: Ghosts" at participating retailers for a suggested retail price of $69.99. This version will include the Xbox 360 digital version of "Call of Duty: Ghosts," as well as the Xbox One digital version of "Call of Duty: Ghosts" &ndash; linked and unlocked via a user&rsquo;s Xbox Live gamertag. More information on how to pre-order at participating retailers will be available in the coming weeks.</span><span style="line-height: 27px; font-size: 18px;"><br />
<br />
</span><span style="font-size: 15px; line-height: 1.5;">Players can also purchase a digital version of "Call of Duty: Ghosts" directly from the Xbox Live Marketplace for a suggested retail price of $59.99. &nbsp;And, when ready to upgrade to Xbox One, you can purchase a digital Xbox One version of "Call of Duty: Ghosts" for a suggested retail price of just $10 on the Xbox Live Marketplace until March 31, 2014.</span><span style="font-size: 15px; line-height: 22px;"><br />
<br />
</span><strong><span style="font-size: 18px;">In-Game Stats and Profiles Transition With You</span><br />
</strong><br />
<span style="font-size: 15px; line-height: 1.5;">When you transition from Xbox 360 to Xbox One your "Call of Duty: Ghosts" stats, unlocks, and more will seamlessly go with you, and be ready when you are. &nbsp;Utilizing your free "Call of Duty" account, available with "Call of Duty: ELITE," you can carry your profile with you from Xbox 360 to Xbox One and back again. It&rsquo;s that easy. &nbsp;And if you don&rsquo;t already have an account, it&rsquo;s really easy to get one for free. Go to </span><a href="http://elite.callofduty.com " target="_blank" style="font-size: 15px; line-height: 1.5;">http://elite.callofduty.com </a><span style="font-size: 15px; line-height: 1.5;">to sign up now or you can sign up in game when you boot up "Call of Duty: Ghosts."</span><strong style="font-size: large;"><br />
</strong>
<br />
<strong><span style="font-size: 18px;">Season Pass Upgrades at No Additional Cost</span><br />
</strong><br />
<span style="font-size: 15px; line-height: 1.5;">Priced at a suggested retail price of $49.99, &pound;34.99, or &euro;49.99, the "Call of Duty: Ghosts" Season Pass will upgrade from Xbox 360 to Xbox One at no additional cost. &nbsp;Offer is valid until May 31, 2014.</span><span style="line-height: 27px; font-size: 18px;"><strong><br />
<br />
</strong></span><span style="font-size: 15px; line-height: 1.5;">The "Call of Duty: Ghosts" Season Pass is now available for pre-order on the Xbox Live Marketplace and at participating PC digital download retailers. The Season Pass includes access to four "Call of Duty: Ghosts" DLC Map Packs, each available first, exclusively, on Xbox and delivering a collection of fresh "Call of Duty: Ghosts" multiplayer content and more. Gamers who purchase the Season Pass will also receive the Team Leader digital content pack that includes a unique multiplayer character head, weapon camo, reticle, player patch, player card and player background.&nbsp;</span>
	</article>
