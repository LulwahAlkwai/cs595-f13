<section class='article-content'>

<p><em>This is a developing story. We will continue to update this article as more information becomes available.</em></p>
<p><strong>UPDATE - 11:22 p.m. ET: A shooting rampage at the Washington Navy Yard on Monday has left 13 people dead, including the gunman, according to <a href="http://bigstory.ap.org/article/12-killed-washington-navy-yard-shooting" target="_blank">multiple</a> <a href="http://www.washingtonpost.com/local/police-search-for-active-shooter-on-grounds-of-washington-navy-yard-in-southeast-dc/2013/09/16/b1d72b9a-1ecb-11e3-b7d1-7153ad47b549_story.html" target="_blank">reports</a>. <br><br>The <a href="http://www.fbi.gov/wanted/seeking-info/aaron-alexis" target="_blank">FBI identified the suspected shooter as Aaron Alexis</a>, 34, whose last-known address was in Fort Worth, Texas.</strong></p>
<p>Police are on the hunt for a suspect or suspects who shot 10 people at the Washington Navy Yard, according to <a href="http://www.washingtonpost.com/local/police-search-for-active-shooter-on-grounds-of-washington-navy-yard-in-southeast-dc/2013/09/16/b1d72b9a-1ecb-11e3-b7d1-7153ad47b549_story.html" target="_blank">reports</a>.
