Twitter Poaches Microsoft Developer Relations Director for Platform Gig

   September 17, 2013 at 10:44 am PT
     * [59]Tweet
     * [60]Share on Facebook
     *
     *
     * Share
     * [61]Print

   Twitter has named Jeff Sandquist as its next director of platform
   partnerships, according to a [62]tweet Sandquist sent on Tuesday. He'll
   fill the position [63]vacated by Ryan Sarver, who until May had worked
   closely with Twitter's ecosystem partners over the past four years.
   Before Twitter, Sandquist worked at Microsoft in various roles for the
   past 16 years, with his most recent title being senior director of
   developer relations.
