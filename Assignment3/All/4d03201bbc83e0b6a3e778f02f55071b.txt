<section class='article-content'>

<p><a href="http://mashable.com/category/apple/">Apple</a> has put up its colorful new smartphone, the <a href="http://mashable.com/category/iphone-5c/">iPhone 5C</a>, up for pre-order. </p>
<p>Once you <a href="http://store.apple.com/us/buy-iphone/iphone5c" target="_blank">choose</a> from one of the five available colors and the storage capacity (16GB is $99, 32GB is $199), you can get the device with a contract from one of three network carriers — AT&amp;T, Sprint or Verizon. </p>
<p>You can also pre-order the iPhone 5C unlocked and contract free, for $549 (16GB) or $649 (32GB). </p>
<div class="see-also"><p>See also: <a href="http://mashable.com/2013/09/11/iphone-5s-iphone-5c-comparison/" data-crackerjax="#post-slider">iPhone 5S vs. iPhone 5C: Which Should You Buy?</a></p></div>
<p>The delivery date for all variants is September 20, which is also when the <a href="http://mashable.com/category/iphone-5s/">iPhone 5S</a> becomes available in stores. </p>
<p>The iPhone 5C is Apple's replacement for the iPhone 5. Besides the colorful appearance (it's available in blue, green, yellow pink and white), and a plastic chassis, it is nearly the same as its predecessor, with a few upgrades — improved FaceTime and support for more networks. </p>
