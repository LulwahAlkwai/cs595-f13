			<p><a href="http://evernote.com/ec"><img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-1476" alt="ec3-banner-610" src="http://blog.evernote.com/tech/files/2013/07/ec3-banner-610.png" width="610" height="102" /></a></p>
<p>We are excited to announce the Evernote Conference, our annual event for developers, designers, and our community of users. Over the past few months, we have crafted a collection of talks, panels, and technical workshops that help you get more out of Evernote and the platform.</p>
<h3><a href="http://evernoteconference13.eventbrite.com/">Reserve your spot today &gt;&gt; </a></h3>
<p>We&#8217;ve built such a great lineup of speakers and workshops that we couldn&#8217;t fit them all into just one day, so this year we&#8217;re expanding the conference to two days.</p>
<p><b>Speakers and panelists include:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Phil Libin &#8211; CEO, Evernote</li>
<li>Loic LeMeur &#8211; Founder, LeWeb</li>
<li>Dave Winer &#8211; Creator of RSS</li>
<li>Rafe Needleman &#8211; Writer and Director of Dev Relations, Evernote</li>
<li>Nate Weiner &#8211; Founder and CEO, Pocket</li>
<li>Eric Migicovsky &#8211; Creator, Pebble</li>
<li>Ben Zotto &#8211; Creator of Penultimate</li>
<li>Randy J Hunt - <i>Creative Director, Etsy</i></li>
<li>Edwin Khodabakchian &#8211; CEO, Feedly <i><br />
</i></li>
<li>Brit Morin &#8211; Founder, Brit + Co.</li>
<li>Seth Hitchings &#8211; VP of Platform Strategy, Evernote</li>
<li><strong><a href="http://evernote.com/ec/#speakers">View full list of speakers &gt;&gt;</a></strong></li>
</ul>
<p>We hope you&#8217;ll join us as we dive deeper into use cases to help you work smarter and live better. We&#8217;re hosting panel discussions on current tech triends led by our peers in the industry. If you&#8217;re looking for more hands-on sessions, we&#8217;ve got you covered with technical and business focused workshops.</p>
<p><b>Workshops and talks include:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Evernote Business API</li>
<li>Building Apps for iOS 7</li>
<li>Designing and Building Local APIs for Android</li>
<li>Evernote API and the Semantic Web</li>
<li>Running your Business with Evernote</li>
<li>Augmented Intelligence</li>
<li>PNG Optimization for iPad apps</li>
<li>Project Coordination Workshops</li>
<li><strong><a href="http://evernote.com/ec/#workshops">View full list of workshops &gt;&gt;</a></strong></li>
</ul>
<p>We&#8217;re working hard to ensure that the two days you spend with us are productive, informative, inspirational and fun. In addition to the talks, we will include company announcements &#8212; You don&#8217;t want to miss it!</p>
<p><b>Details</b><br />
When: Thursday and Friday, September 26th-27th, 2013<br />
Where: Concourse Exhibition Hall &#8211; 635 8th Street, San Francisco, CA<br />
Price: Early Bird tickets $350</p>
<h3><a href="http://evernoteconference13.eventbrite.com/">REGISTER HERE</a></h3>
