Apple's iPhone 5C Now Available for Pre-Order

   2.3k
   Shares
   [44]Share
   [45]Tweet
   [46]Share
   What's This?
   Iphone-5c1
   [47]Stan-schroeder
   By Stan Schroeder2013-09-13 07:28:49 UTC

   [48]Apple has put up its colorful new smartphone, the [49]iPhone 5C, up
   for pre-order.

   Once you [50]choose from one of the five available colors and the
   storage capacity (16GB is $99, 32GB is $199), you can get the device
   with a contract from one of three network carriers -- AT&T, Sprint or
   Verizon.

   You can also pre-order the iPhone 5C unlocked and contract free, for
   $549 (16GB) or $649 (32GB).

   See also: [51]iPhone 5S vs. iPhone 5C: Which Should You Buy?

   The delivery date for all variants is September 20, which is also when
   the [52]iPhone 5S becomes available in stores.

   The iPhone 5C is Apple's replacement for the iPhone 5. Besides the
   colorful appearance (it's available in blue, green, yellow pink and
   white), and a plastic chassis, it is nearly the same as its
   predecessor, with a few upgrades -- improved FaceTime and support for
   more networks.
