                  Person Finder: [3]Boston Marathon Explosions

   Person Finder is no longer active for the Boston Marathon bombings. We
   thank everyone who contributed to the response. See the [4]Red Cross
   for advice on how to find or offer help, or go to [5]Red Cross Safe &
   Well if you want to report or register missing persons.
     * Boston Mayor's Hotline for families of victims: 617-635-4500
     * Boston Police line for witnesses who may have information:
       800-494-8477

   In accordance with our [6]standard practice, the data entered into
   Person Finder will be deleted to protect the privacy of everyone
   affected by the event. Our hearts are with you, Boston.

   PLEASE NOTE: On active Person Finder sites, all data entered is
   available to the public and usable by anyone. Google does not review or
   verify the accuracy of this data.
