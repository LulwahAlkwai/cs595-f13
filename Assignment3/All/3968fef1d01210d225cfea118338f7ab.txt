<p>Tuesday was a big day for <a href="http://mashable.com/category/apple/">Apple</a>.</p>
<p>The Cupertino, Calif.-based company is set to ship its <a href="http://mashable.com/2013/09/10/apple-700-million-ios/" data-crackerjax="#post-slider">700 millionth iOS device</a> next month and held an event at its headquarters to show off its new products and the next generation of its mobile software, iOS 7.</p>
<div class="see-also"><p>See also: <a href="http://mashable.com/2013/09/10/iphone-5s-versus-the-competition/" data-crackerjax="#post-slider">iPhone 5S vs. the Competition</a></p></div>
<p>If you missed out on our <a href="http://mashable.com/2013/09/09/apple-iphone-event-live-blog/" data-crackerjax="#post-slider">live blog</a> during the event, here’s a quick rundown on all of Apple’s big announcements.</p>
<h2>iOS 7</h2>
<p><a href="http://mashable.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/09/iOS7_edited-1.jpg"><img src="http://rack.3.mshcdn.com/media/ZgkyMDEzLzA5LzEwL2RlL2lPUzdfZWRpdGVkLjE5MzRiLmpwZwpwCXRodW1iCTEyMDB4OTYwMD4/ad9fbd02/b69/iOS7_edited-1.jpg" alt="iOS7_edited-1" class="" data-width="1900" data-fragment="m!50c6" data-image="http://rack.3.mshcdn.com/media/ZgkyMDEzLzA5LzEwL2RlL2lPUzdfZWRpdGVkLjE5MzRiLmpwZwpwCXRodW1iCTEyMDB4OTYwMD4/ad9fbd02/b69/iOS7_edited-1.jpg" data-micro="1"></a></p>
<p>By now, you have probably seen a glimpse of iOS 7, which was officially announced during Apple’s Worldwide Developers Conference (<a href="http://mashable.com/category/wwdc/">WWDC</a>) in June. On Tuesday, Apple revealed that it would release the new operating system on Sept. 18.</p>
<p>Craig Federighi, senior vice president of software engineering at Apple, provided an overview of some of the new features we can expect with iOS 7, including a new Control Center, Notification Center and multitasking view. He also showed off some new ringtones for the operating system and new system alert sounds.</p>
<p>A new “Share Sheet” within iOS 7 makes it easy to share content with friends on <a href="http://mashable.com/category/twitter/">Twitter</a> and other social networks, and AirDrop lets you virtually drop files onto a friend’s iPhone nearby.</p>
<p>iTunes Radio <a href="http://mashable.com/2013/09/10/itunes-radio-sept-18/" data-crackerjax="#post-slider">will also launch with iOS 7</a> on Sept. 18.</p>
<p>Check out <a href="http://mashable.com/2013/06/12/ios-7-hands-on/" data-crackerjax="#post-slider">our hands-on with iOS 7</a> for a more in-depth look at the new operating system.</p>
<h2>Two New iPhones</h2>
<p><a href="http://mashable.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/09/jxyh3LEegskRliMLRAlihAJB7_JW55hOuzuqzvF3wuA.jpg"><img src="http://rack.1.mshcdn.com/media/ZgkyMDEzLzA5LzEwLzM3L2p4eWgzTEVlZ3NrLmYzMTM1LmpwZwpwCXRodW1iCTEyMDB4OTYwMD4/5fbe403b/e44/jxyh3LEegskRliMLRAlihAJB7_JW55hOuzuqzvF3wuA.jpg" alt="jxyh3LEegskRliMLRAlihAJB7_JW55hOuzuqzvF3wuA" class="" data-width="1024" data-fragment="m!3a43" data-image="http://rack.1.mshcdn.com/media/ZgkyMDEzLzA5LzEwLzM3L2p4eWgzTEVlZ3NrLmYzMTM1LmpwZwpwCXRodW1iCTEyMDB4OTYwMD4/5fbe403b/e44/jxyh3LEegskRliMLRAlihAJB7_JW55hOuzuqzvF3wuA.jpg" data-micro="1"></a></p>
<p>As expected, Apple also announced two new iPhones: the iPhone 5S and 5C. Both phones will be available in stores on Sept. 20, and you can preorder the 5C starting Sept. 13.</p>
<p>The <a href="http://mashable.com/2013/09/10/apple-unveils-iphone-5s/" data-crackerjax="#post-slider">iPhone 5S</a> will be available in silver, gold and space gray, which looks a lot like this year’s black. The phone has a new A7 processor re-engineered for 64-bit architecture, translating to a much faster phone — one that’s capable of running much more complex software. </p>
<p>One example shown on stage was Epic Game’s <a href="http://mashable.com/2013/09/10/infinity-blade-iii/" data-crackerjax="#post-slider"><em>Infinity Blade III</em></a>, which uses the company’s Unreal Engine to create exceptionally immersive and vivid gameplay on mobile.</p>
<p>The handset boasts an improved camera with a f/2.2 aperture for taking better photos in low light as well as a dual-LED flash for creating the perfect natural color balance in shots. A panorama mode can take a 28-megapixel shot, and a new burst mode snaps 10 still pictures in one second.</p>
<p>A <a href="http://mashable.com/2013/09/10/apple-2/" data-crackerjax="#post-slider">fingerprint scanner</a> on the phone's home button can be used in place of the traditional passcode for the phone. Owners can also use it the scanner to sign into iTunes to buy apps and media.</p>
<p><a href="http://mashable.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/09/Apple-event-175.jpg"><img src="http://rack.0.mshcdn.com/media/ZgkyMDEzLzA5LzEwLzYwL0FwcGxlZXZlbnQxLmJkOTA4LmpwZwpwCXRodW1iCTEyMDB4OTYwMD4/471e5bff/82d/Apple-event-175.jpg" alt="Apple-event-175" class="" data-width="5760" data-fragment="m!1234" data-image="http://rack.0.mshcdn.com/media/ZgkyMDEzLzA5LzEwLzYwL0FwcGxlZXZlbnQxLmJkOTA4LmpwZwpwCXRodW1iCTEyMDB4OTYwMD4/471e5bff/82d/Apple-event-175.jpg" data-micro="1"></a></p>
<p>The phone is the same price as the current iPhone 5: $199 for the 16GB version, $299 for 32GB and $399 for 64GB.</p>
<p>The 5C has many of the same capabilities as the iPhone 5, but the rear of the 5C is replaced by a single piece of colored polycarbonate. The iPhone 5C will come in vibrant green, blue, pink, yellow and white, and will be priced at $100 less than the current iPhone 5, starting at $99 for the 16GB version.</p>
<p><a href="http://mashable.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/09/lLa20xWomb3orM5z2bM88QUFqx6vzlbp9Uv62n8qbr4.jpg"><img src="http://rack.1.mshcdn.com/media/ZgkyMDEzLzA5LzEwLzk2L2xMYTIweFdvbWIzLmU4YzliLmpwZwpwCXRodW1iCTEyMDB4OTYwMD4/4436d68e/138/lLa20xWomb3orM5z2bM88QUFqx6vzlbp9Uv62n8qbr4.jpg" alt="lLa20xWomb3orM5z2bM88QUFqx6vzlbp9Uv62n8qbr4" class="" data-width="1024" data-fragment="m!e0e7" data-image="http://rack.1.mshcdn.com/media/ZgkyMDEzLzA5LzEwLzk2L2xMYTIweFdvbWIzLmU4YzliLmpwZwpwCXRodW1iCTEyMDB4OTYwMD4/4436d68e/138/lLa20xWomb3orM5z2bM88QUFqx6vzlbp9Uv62n8qbr4.jpg" data-micro="1"></a></p>
<p>Apple offers cases for both phones. For the 5S, you can purchase leather cases in subdued colors that cover the volume buttons on the phone for $49. The 5C has more vibrantly colored cases with rows of holes cut out on the back to allow the phone's color to shine through for $29.</p>
<p>With the launch of the phones Apple has discontinued the iPhone 5. The iPhone 4S will still remain available as a free phone with a new two-year contract.</p>
<h2>Free Apps</h2>
<p><a href="http://mashable.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/09/MdOEr5hbFF21zLPTRgT2uSjG7KVGNzZixOUmDxbac1g.jpg"><img src="http://rack.1.mshcdn.com/media/ZgkyMDEzLzA5LzEwLzE1L01kT0VyNWhiRkYyLmIxMGFmLmpwZwpwCXRodW1iCTEyMDB4OTYwMD4/ed212f1a/053/MdOEr5hbFF21zLPTRgT2uSjG7KVGNzZixOUmDxbac1g.jpg" alt="MdOEr5hbFF21zLPTRgT2uSjG7KVGNzZixOUmDxbac1g" class="" data-width="1024" data-fragment="m!76d5" data-image="http://rack.1.mshcdn.com/media/ZgkyMDEzLzA5LzEwLzE1L01kT0VyNWhiRkYyLmIxMGFmLmpwZwpwCXRodW1iCTEyMDB4OTYwMD4/ed212f1a/053/MdOEr5hbFF21zLPTRgT2uSjG7KVGNzZixOUmDxbac1g.jpg" data-micro="1"></a></p>
<p>Apple made <a href="http://mashable.com/2013/09/10/imovie-iwork-free/" data-crackerjax="#post-slider">several of its popular apps free</a> on Tuesday, too. Now, whenever you purchase a new iPhone, <a href="http://mashable.com/category/ipad/">iPad</a> or fifth-generation iPod touch, you can download iWork, Keynote, Pages, Numbers, iPhoto and iMovie for free. These apps are normally priced between $4.99 and $9.99 each.</p>
<p>Do you plan to purchase an iPhone 5S or 5C? Let us know your thoughts in the comments.</p>
