   Sure, it's been two years since the last space shuttle launch. And
   NASA's budget is tight. And the program is [206]a political football.

   Nevertheless, [207]as I write in today's Chronicle (premium), that
   doesn't mean there's nothing to look forward to in spaceflight. Far
   from it.

   So with the help of some Twitter friends -- the Planetary Society's
   [208]Emily Lakdawalla, Caltech's [209]Mike Brown, former astronaut
   [210]Leroy Chiao, SETI's [211]Franck Marchis and aspiring astronaut
   [212]Abigail Harrison -- I've compiled a list of five cool upcoming
   missions.
