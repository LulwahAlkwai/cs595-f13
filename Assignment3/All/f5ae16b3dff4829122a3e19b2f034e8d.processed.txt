Leaked Images Show Next-Generation Kindle Fire HD

   1.0k
   Shares
   [44]Share
   [45]Tweet
   [46]Share
   What's This?
   Bgr-amazon-kindle-fire-2-6
   [47]Nycmsh
   By Adario Strange2013-09-12 01:59:14 UTC

   A new set of leaked images appear to reveal the design for Amazon's
   next-generation [48]Kindle Fire HD tablet.

   First published by [49]BGR, the photos show a radically modified
   exterior for the high-end tablet, which is accented by angular edges;
   this is a distinct departure from the smooth, rounded edges of its
   predecessor (see photo, above).

   SEE ALSO: [50]Kindle Fire HD Teardown Reveals Repairs Are (Relatively)
   Easy

   In some ways, the new design (which is partially obscured, presumably
   to protect the source of the leak) is reminiscent of the old [51]HTC
   Touch Diamond smartphone, a minor hit with design-centric mobile users
   a few years ago.

   In a previous [52]report, BGR offered unconfirmed details on the specs
   for Amazon's upcoming 7-inch and 8.9-inch Kindle Fire HD tablets.
   Citing "trusted sources," the site claimed that the 7-inch Kindle Fire
   HD will feature a 1,920 x 1,200-pixel display, 2 GB of RAM and a
   front-facing camera.

   The report added that the 8.9-inch version will include a 2,560 x 1,600
   display, a front-facing camera and a rear-facing camera. Both tablets
   will reportedly weigh significantly less than previous models.

   Amazon did not immediately respond to a request for comment.
